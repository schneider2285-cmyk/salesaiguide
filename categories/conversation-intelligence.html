Best Conversation Intelligence AI Tools for B2B Sales Teams

Conversation intelligence tools record, transcribe, and analyze every sales call your team takes. They use AI to surface patterns across thousands of conversations — identifying which talk tracks win deals, where reps lose momentum, what competitors get mentioned, and how deals are progressing based on what buyers actually say. For sales leaders, this means coaching based on data instead of gut instinct.

The market is effectively a two-horse race. Gong is the category leader with 5,800+ G2 reviews and the deepest analytics capabilities. It goes beyond call recording into full revenue intelligence — tracking deal health, forecasting outcomes, and providing board-ready pipeline analytics. Chorus, now owned by ZoomInfo, is a strong alternative that integrates tightly with ZoomInfo's data ecosystem. For teams already invested in ZoomInfo for prospecting, Chorus offers a more unified experience at what is generally a lower price point.

Both platforms require custom pricing, which typically scales based on the number of recorded users. Key decision factors include integration depth with your CRM and dialer, the sophistication of AI-powered insights, and whether you need standalone conversation analytics or prefer it bundled with a broader revenue intelligence or data platform. Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks including CRM integration and team onboarding.
