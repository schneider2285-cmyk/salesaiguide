Best Data Enrichment AI Tools for B2B Sales Teams

Data enrichment tools fill in the gaps in your CRM and lead lists. They take a basic lead record — maybe just a name and company — and layer on firmographic data (company size, revenue, industry), technographic data (what software they use), contact details (verified emails, direct dials), and buying intent signals. The result is a complete picture of every prospect before your reps even pick up the phone.

Two platforms stand out in this category. Clay has become the go-to for RevOps teams running complex outbound workflows. It aggregates data from 50+ providers through a spreadsheet-like interface, uses AI to research prospects, and chains enrichment steps together without code. At $149/month, it is not cheap, but the depth of what you can automate is unmatched. Clearbit, now part of HubSpot, focuses on real-time enrichment that runs automatically inside your CRM. It is the natural choice for HubSpot users who want lead data enriched the moment a form is submitted or a new contact enters the system.

The key decision factors are your existing tech stack, volume of records to enrich, and whether you need one-time batch enrichment or continuous real-time updates. Clay excels at complex, multi-step enrichment workflows for outbound teams. Clearbit excels at always-on enrichment that keeps your CRM data fresh without manual effort.
