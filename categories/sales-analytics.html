Best Sales Analytics AI Tools for B2B Sales Teams

Sales analytics tools go beyond what your CRM's built-in reporting can deliver. They use AI to forecast revenue with higher accuracy, inspect pipeline health at the deal level, capture rep activity automatically, and surface buying intent signals that tell you which accounts are ready to engage. For revenue leaders who need to call the number with confidence, these platforms are essential infrastructure.

Clari leads the revenue intelligence category, focusing on forecast accuracy and pipeline inspection. It pulls data from your CRM, email, and calendar to build a comprehensive view of every deal, then uses AI to flag risks and predict outcomes. People.ai specializes in automatic activity capture â€” it logs every email, meeting, and call without reps lifting a finger, then maps that activity to accounts and opportunities to show where engagement is strong or dropping off. 6sense takes a different approach entirely, using intent data and predictive analytics to identify which accounts are actively researching solutions before they ever fill out a form.

All three platforms use custom pricing, which typically scales based on the number of CRM users or accounts being tracked. The key decision factors are what problem you need to solve first: forecast accuracy (Clari), activity visibility (People.ai), or demand identification (6sense). Many enterprise organizations end up using two or more of these tools because they solve fundamentally different problems.
