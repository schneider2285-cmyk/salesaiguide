What Is Sales Analytics?

Sales analytics platforms sit on top of your CRM and engagement tools to surface the patterns that drive revenue. They answer questions your CRM can’t easily answer on its own: which deals are actually going to close, which reps are executing the winning playbook, where pipeline is leaking, and whether this quarter’s forecast is real or optimistic. The category ranges from AI forecasting engines like Clari to intent data platforms like 6sense that identify accounts before they raise their hand.

Who Needs a Dedicated Analytics Platform?

Teams under 20 reps typically get enough insight from CRM native reports. The tipping point for a dedicated analytics platform is usually when leadership can no longer trust the forecast from rollup spreadsheets, when you have enough CRM data to train a predictive model, or when the cost of forecast misses exceeds the cost of the platform.
