Best Sales Content AI Tools for B2B Sales Teams

Sales content tools help reps create better outreach, manage collateral, and use personalized media to stand out in crowded inboxes. This category spans three distinct use cases: AI email coaching that improves reply rates, video messaging that adds a personal touch to prospecting, and content management platforms that ensure reps always have the right materials at their fingertips. Each tool addresses a different bottleneck in the sales process.

Lavender is the standout for AI email coaching — it scores your emails in real time, provides specific improvement suggestions, and shows you a mobile preview before you send. At $29/month, it pays for itself if it lifts reply rates even modestly. Vidyard focuses on video messaging, letting reps record personalized videos and screen shares that embed directly in emails. Also at $29/month, it is popular with teams that find video outperforms plain text for certain buyer personas. Seismic operates at the enterprise level as a full content management and sales enablement platform with custom pricing.

The key decision factors depend on your biggest content gap. If your reps send a lot of email and reply rates are low, start with Lavender. If you want to differentiate outreach with video, go with Vidyard. If your organization needs centralized content management with analytics on what collateral gets used and what converts, Seismic is the enterprise play. Many teams use two or more of these tools together since they address different problems.
