What Is Sales Engagement?

Sales engagement platforms centralize the way reps interact with prospects across email, phone, and social channels. Instead of toggling between a CRM, a dialer, and an email client, reps work from a single sequencing engine that automates follow-ups, logs every touchpoint, and surfaces analytics on what is working. The category grew out of the need to coordinate multi-touch cadences at scale without losing personalization or letting prospects fall through the cracks.

Key Differences Between Platforms

  • Outreach vs Salesloft: Both are enterprise-grade platforms. Outreach has stronger pipeline management; Salesloft has better conversation intelligence built in.
  • Groove: Best for teams that live in Salesforce and don’t want a separate platform. Activity syncs back without rep effort.
  • Recapped.io: Solves a different problem — post-demo deal execution rather than outbound sequencing. Strong fit for enterprise AEs managing buying committees.
