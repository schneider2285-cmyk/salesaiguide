What Is CRM & Pipeline Management?

CRM and pipeline management tools give sales teams a structured way to track deals from first touch to closed-won. A CRM stores contacts and companies in a structured database; add pipeline management, and you get a visual system for moving deals through stages, assigning tasks, and forecasting revenue accurately. Modern CRMs also automate manual data entry by capturing emails, calls, and meetings directly from rep activity.

How to Choose

  • Team size and complexity: HubSpot and Pipedrive fit teams under 50 reps well. Salesforce scales for complex enterprise workflows but requires dedicated admin resources.
  • Inside sales vs. field sales: Close is purpose-built for inside sales teams that live on the phone. Salesforce handles complex territory and account hierarchies better.
  • Integration requirements: Check native integrations with your existing marketing stack, enrichment tools, and sales engagement platforms before committing.
