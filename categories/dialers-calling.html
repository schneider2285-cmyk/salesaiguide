Best Dialers & Calling AI Tools for B2B Sales Teams

Sales dialers and cloud calling platforms are essential for teams where phone outreach drives pipeline. Modern dialers go far beyond click-to-call — they offer AI-powered transcription, real-time coaching prompts, voicemail detection, parallel dialing to maximize connect rates, and deep CRM integration that logs every call automatically. For SDR teams that make 50-200+ dials per day, the right dialer directly impacts productivity and connect rates.

Dialpad stands out for its AI capabilities, offering real-time transcription, sentiment analysis, and live coaching suggestions during calls — all starting at $27/month. Aircall focuses on being a reliable, well-designed cloud phone system with strong integrations at $30/month, making it popular with teams that want great UX without overcomplicating things. Orum takes a fundamentally different approach with parallel dialing — it dials multiple prospects simultaneously and connects reps only when someone picks up, dramatically increasing the number of live conversations per hour.

The key decision factors are calling volume, the importance of AI features, and whether you need a general business phone system (Dialpad, Aircall) or a specialized high-volume sales dialer (Orum). Teams doing fewer than 50 calls per day may be fine with the built-in dialer in their CRM or engagement platform. Teams doing 100+ calls per day will see massive productivity gains from a dedicated dialer, especially one with parallel capabilities.
