When to Choose LeadIQ

✓ Choose LeadIQ if:

  • LinkedIn is your primary prospecting channel and you live in Sales Navigator
  • One-click CRM capture matters and you want contacts pushed directly to Salesforce/HubSpot
  • You want an AI email writer built into your prospecting workflow (Scribe)
  • Enterprise compliance is needed with SOC 2 and SSO on Enterprise plans
  • You prospect primarily in North America where LeadIQ's data coverage is strongest

✗ Skip LeadIQ if:

  • Direct dial phone numbers are your top priority
  • You need EU data residency for GDPR compliance
  • Your prospecting extends beyond LinkedIn to company websites and other sources

Key strengths: LinkedIn capture, one-click CRM sync, AI email writer, SOC 2 certified, Sales Navigator integration

Migration Notes

Lusha → LeadIQ

Effort: Low (1-2 days)

Export contacts from Lusha and import into your CRM. Install LeadIQ's Chrome extension and connect to your CRM. The main adjustment is shifting from Lusha's search-first approach to LeadIQ's LinkedIn-first workflow.

What you gain: LinkedIn-to-CRM capture, AI email writer (Scribe), deeper CRM integration

What you lose: Direct dial accuracy, simpler multi-site extension, EU data residency

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep: Lusha's Rep score of 70 slightly edges out LeadIQ's 68, reflecting its simpler interface and better direct dials. If your workflow is LinkedIn-centric, LeadIQ's one-click capture may save you more time despite the slightly lower score.

If you're a sales leader: LeadIQ's Org score of 62 vs Lusha's 60 reflects its stronger CRM integration and enterprise features. For teams that live in LinkedIn Sales Navigator, LeadIQ's workflow efficiency is worth the investment. For teams that prioritize simplicity and phone outreach, Lusha is the better pick.

If EU compliance matters: Lusha wins with native EU data residency. LeadIQ is US-only, which may be a blocker for European organizations with strict GDPR requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LeadIQ or Lusha better for LinkedIn prospecting?

LeadIQ is better for LinkedIn-centric prospecting. Its Chrome extension is purpose-built for LinkedIn Sales Navigator with one-click capture to CRM. Lusha's extension works across LinkedIn and other sites but the LinkedIn-to-CRM workflow is less seamless than LeadIQ's.

Which has more accurate phone numbers?

Lusha is generally stronger for direct dial phone numbers, especially in European markets. LeadIQ provides good phone data but users report more limited mobile number coverage. If direct dials are your top priority, Lusha has the edge.

Can I use LeadIQ or Lusha as an Apollo alternative?

Both can partially replace Apollo for contact data, but neither includes built-in email sequences or a dialer. LeadIQ is the better Apollo alternative if you need CRM sync and LinkedIn workflow. Lusha is better if you need simple contact lookups. For a full Apollo replacement, you'd pair either tool with a separate outreach platform.
