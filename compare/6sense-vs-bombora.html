Migration Notes

Bombora → 6sense

Effort: High (4-8 weeks)

Moving from Bombora's data feed to 6sense's full platform is a significant undertaking. You'll need to rebuild audiences, scoring models, and integrations in 6sense. Expect a ramp-up period as the platform learns your account signals and your team adapts to the new workflow.

What you gain: Full ABM orchestration, predictive analytics, built-in advertising, multi-source intent

What you lose: Cooperative publisher data model, lower cost, integration flexibility

6sense → Bombora

Effort: Medium (2-4 weeks)

Transitioning from 6sense to Bombora means replacing a full platform with a data feed. You'll need an alternative ABM tool (Demandbase, HubSpot, etc.) to orchestrate campaigns. Export your account lists and rebuild intent-based workflows in your new stack.

What you gain: Lower cost, cooperative intent signals, flexibility to choose your own ABM platform

What you lose: All-in-one orchestration, predictive buying-stage models, built-in advertising

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep or small team: Neither tool is designed for individual use. Both are organization-level investments. If your team is exploring intent data for the first time, Bombora's lower entry point and simpler integration make it a better starting place.

If you're a sales or marketing leader: 6sense's Org score of 76 reflects its comprehensive platform capabilities. If you need a single platform for ABM orchestration with built-in intent, predictions, and advertising, 6sense is the category leader. If you already have ABM tools and need focused intent data to enhance them, Bombora's Org score of 68 represents strong signal quality at a more accessible price point.

Key consideration: Compare total cost of ownership. 6sense replaces multiple tools but costs $60K-120K+/yr. Bombora at $25K-60K/yr plus your existing ABM platform may end up at similar total spend with more flexibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 6sense or Bombora better for a mid-market ABM program?

It depends on what you already have. If you need a full ABM orchestration platform (audience building, advertising, predictive scoring), 6sense is the all-in-one choice. If you already have an ABM platform like Demandbase or HubSpot and just need intent signals, Bombora's data feed integrates into your existing stack at a lower cost.

Can I use 6sense and Bombora together?

Yes, though it's less common. Some teams use Bombora's cooperative intent data alongside 6sense's own multi-source signals for broader signal coverage. However, 6sense already includes its own intent data, so the overlap may not justify the combined cost for most teams.

Which tool has better intent data accuracy?

6sense uses multi-source signals (web, ads, keywords, third-party) for broader coverage. Bombora uses a cooperative model from 5,000+ B2B content sites for deeper content consumption signals. 6sense is generally considered more accurate for predicting buying stage, while Bombora excels at topic-level intent from content engagement.
