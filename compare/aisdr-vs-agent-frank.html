The Verdict

If you're an individual rep or small team: Agent Frank's $499/mo price point and multichannel reach make it the pragmatic choice for reps who want to automate outreach across email and LinkedIn without breaking the bank. AiSDR's Rep score of 55 vs Agent Frank's 52 is a narrow gap that may not justify the ~50% price premium.

If you're a sales leader evaluating AI SDR tools: AiSDR's slightly higher Org score (48 vs 45) reflects better AI depth and pricing transparency. If your team values lead qualification quality and email-focused outreach, AiSDR delivers more refined AI. If multichannel reach and budget matter more, Agent Frank offers more channel coverage per dollar.

Important caveat: Both tools are very early-stage with limited reviews and track records. Run pilot programs before committing. Neither is suitable for enterprise-level deployments requiring compliance features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AiSDR or Agent Frank better for a bootstrapped startup?

Agent Frank is the more budget-friendly option at ~$499/mo compared to AiSDR's ~$750/mo. However, AiSDR offers more transparent pricing and slightly better AI scoring. For bootstrapped startups where every dollar counts, Agent Frank's lower entry point and multichannel approach (email + LinkedIn) may deliver more value per dollar.

Can AiSDR or Agent Frank handle enterprise-level outbound?

Neither tool is built for enterprise-level outbound at scale. Both lack SOC 2 certification, SSO, formal SLAs, and robust RBAC. They are best suited for startups and SMBs looking to automate initial SDR outreach. Enterprise teams should consider more established platforms like Outreach, Salesloft, or the premium AI SDRs (11x.ai, Artisan).

Which tool has better email deliverability?

AiSDR currently has a slight edge on deliverability based on user feedback, though both tools are early-stage. Agent Frank (part of Salesforge) has reported deliverability concerns in community discussions. Both tools require proper email warmup and domain setup to achieve good inbox placement rates.
