Migration Notes

Avoma → Gong

Effort: Medium (1-2 weeks)

Historical call recordings can be imported, but Avoma's AI summaries and coaching data don't transfer. Plan to re-train teams on Gong's more complex interface and rebuild coaching scorecards. The deeper analytics require manager training to fully leverage.

What you gain: Deeper AI insights, deal intelligence, pipeline analytics, enterprise compliance

What you lose: Built-in meeting scheduler, transparent pricing, simpler UX

Gong → Avoma

Effort: Low-Medium (3-5 days)

Export call recordings and import into Avoma. The transition is simpler because Avoma's interface is more intuitive. However, teams accustomed to Gong's deep analytics will notice the reduced depth in coaching insights and deal intelligence.

What you gain: 40-50% cost savings, built-in scheduler, transparent pricing

What you lose: Advanced coaching scorecards, deal boards, pipeline risk analysis

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep or small team: Start with Avoma. The Starter plan at $19/mo gives you AI transcription and summaries that cover most daily needs. Gong's value is unlocked at the org level, not the individual level.

If you're a sales leader or VP of Sales: The decision comes down to team size and budget. Gong's Org score of 78 reflects its unmatched analytics depth for enterprise teams. If you have 100+ reps and the budget to justify $30K+ annually, Gong's coaching and deal intelligence deliver measurable ROI. For teams under 50 reps, Avoma's $49-79/seat covers 80% of needs at half the cost.

If budget is your primary constraint: Avoma is the clear winner. Its transparent pricing and tiered plans let you scale from $19/user to $129/user as needs grow, without opaque negotiations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gong worth the price compared to Avoma?

Gong is worth it for enterprise teams that need the deepest conversation analytics, AI coaching scorecards, and deal intelligence. At ~$100-150/seat/mo vs Avoma's $49-79/seat/mo, Gong costs 2-3x more but delivers significantly deeper insights. Mid-market teams often find Avoma covers 80% of their needs at a fraction of the cost.

Can Avoma replace Gong for a growing sales team?

Avoma can replace Gong for teams under 50 reps that primarily need meeting transcription, AI summaries, and basic coaching. Where Avoma falls short is advanced deal intelligence, pipeline analytics, and the depth of AI coaching insights that enterprise teams rely on from Gong.

Which tool has better CRM integration?

Gong has deeper CRM integration with bi-directional Salesforce and HubSpot sync, automatic activity logging, and deal board overlays. Avoma integrates with major CRMs but with less depth. For teams where CRM data hygiene is critical, Gong's native sync is stronger.
