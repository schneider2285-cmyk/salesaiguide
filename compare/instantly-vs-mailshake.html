When to Choose Instantly.ai

✓ Choose Instantly if:

  • Email volume is your priority and you need to send 5K-100K+ emails/month
  • Deliverability is critical and you want built-in warmup + domain rotation
  • You're running multiple domains and need unlimited email account connections
  • Budget efficiency matters with per-workspace rather than per-seat pricing
  • You already have phone/LinkedIn tools and only need a dedicated email engine

✗ Skip Instantly if:

  • You need multi-channel outreach (phone + email + LinkedIn) in one tool
  • Your team is non-technical and needs the simplest possible interface
  • Enterprise compliance (SOC 2, SSO) is required by your procurement team

Key strengths: Deliverability infrastructure, unlimited accounts, built-in warmup, high-volume sending, affordable pricing

What you gain: Better deliverability, unlimited accounts, lower cost at scale

The Verdict

If you're an individual SDR: Choose based on your outreach style. If you're email-first and sending high volume, Instantly's Rep score of 70 and deliverability tools make it the clear winner. If you mix phone calls with email, Mailshake's built-in dialer saves you from managing a separate tool.

If you're a sales leader: Instantly's per-workspace pricing is dramatically cheaper for teams. A 5-person team pays $77.6/mo on Instantly vs $375/mo on Mailshake. However, if your team needs multi-channel sequences, Mailshake's all-in-one approach reduces tool management overhead.

If deliverability is your top concern: Instantly wins decisively. Its built-in warmup, unlimited email accounts, and domain rotation are purpose-built for keeping emails out of spam folders at scale.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Instantly or Mailshake better for beginners?

Mailshake is better for beginners. Its interface is simpler, the learning curve is minimal, and it includes a built-in phone dialer for multi-channel outreach. Instantly has more powerful deliverability features but requires understanding email warmup, domain rotation, and account management to use effectively.

Which tool has better email deliverability?

Instantly has significantly better deliverability infrastructure. Its built-in email warmup, unlimited account connections, and automated domain rotation are purpose-built for high-volume cold email. Mailshake relies on third-party warmup tools and doesn't offer the same level of deliverability optimization.

Can I use Instantly and Mailshake for multi-channel outreach?

Mailshake supports multi-channel outreach with email, phone dialer, and LinkedIn tasks in one workflow. Instantly is primarily email-only and doesn't include a built-in dialer or LinkedIn integration. If multi-channel is important, Mailshake has the advantage.
