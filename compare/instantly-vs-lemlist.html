When to Choose Instantly.ai

✓ Choose Instantly if:

  • Email volume is your strategy and you need to send at scale across unlimited accounts
  • Deliverability is your top concern and you need the best warmup and rotation infrastructure
  • You're an agency managing outreach for multiple clients from one dashboard
  • Budget is tight and you need the lowest cost per email sent
  • You already have LinkedIn tools (like Expandi or Dripify) and only need email covered

✗ Skip Instantly if:

  • You need multichannel sequences with LinkedIn, phone, and email in one tool
  • Personalized images and custom landing pages are core to your outreach strategy
  • Your enterprise procurement team requires SSO, SOC 2, or formal SLAs

Key strengths: Unlimited email accounts, best-in-class warmup network, Unibox unified inbox, agency-friendly dashboard, cost-effective at scale

Migration Notes

Lemlist → Instantly

Effort: Low (2-3 days)

Export Lemlist campaigns and contacts as CSV, then import into Instantly. Rebuild email sequences in Instantly's campaign builder and connect your email accounts. The main adjustment is losing multichannel steps (LinkedIn, landing pages) and adapting to a volume-focused approach.

What you gain: Unlimited accounts, better deliverability, lower cost at scale

What you lose: Personalized images, LinkedIn automation, multichannel sequences, EU hosting

Instantly → Lemlist

Effort: Low-Medium (3-5 days)

Export Instantly contacts and import into Lemlist. Rebuild sequences adding multichannel steps. The biggest change is adjusting to per-seat pricing and email account limits, which may require rethinking your account rotation strategy.

What you gain: Personalized images, LinkedIn automation, custom landing pages, EU hosting

What you lose: Unlimited email accounts, superior deliverability infrastructure, lower cost at volume

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep or agency: Instantly is the better choice for pure cold email. Its Rep score of 70 reflects the value of unlimited accounts, superior deliverability, and lower cost. If you're sending 500+ emails per day and care about inbox placement above all else, Instantly is built for you.

If you're a sales leader running multichannel campaigns: Lemlist's personalization features and LinkedIn integration create a differentiated outreach experience. If your strategy depends on creative, targeted campaigns rather than raw volume, Lemlist's multichannel approach delivers higher reply rates per touch.

The hybrid approach: Some teams use Instantly for high-volume top-of-funnel email outreach and Lemlist for targeted, personalized campaigns to high-value accounts. This lets you maximize volume where it matters while investing in quality where it counts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Instantly or Lemlist better for cold email beginners?

Instantly is generally better for beginners focused on cold email. Its built-in warmup, unlimited email accounts, and Unibox make it simple to scale volume. Lemlist has a steeper learning curve but rewards users who invest time in personalization features like custom images and landing pages.

Does Lemlist support LinkedIn automation?

Yes, Lemlist offers native LinkedIn automation on its Multichannel Expert plan ($99/mo). You can add LinkedIn connection requests, messages, and profile visits as steps in your sequences. Instantly is email-only and does not support LinkedIn or other channels.

Which tool has better email deliverability?

Instantly has a stronger reputation for deliverability. Its unlimited email account rotation, built-in warmup network, and deliverability-first architecture are consistently praised by users. Lemlist offers Lemwarm for warmup but users report it is less effective than Instantly's system for high-volume sending.
