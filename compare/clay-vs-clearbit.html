When to Choose Clay

✓ Choose Clay if:

  • Data quality is your #1 priority and you need multi-source waterfall enrichment
  • You use a CRM other than HubSpot (Salesforce, Pipedrive, etc.) and need flexible integrations
  • You want AI-powered research with Claygent for deep account intelligence
  • You're consolidating data tools and want to replace 3-4 separate subscriptions
  • You have RevOps resources to build and maintain complex enrichment workflows

✗ Skip Clay if:

  • You're a HubSpot-only team wanting zero-config enrichment
  • Your team isn't comfortable with spreadsheet-style workflow builders
  • You need a predictable monthly cost without credit management

Key strengths: 50+ data providers, AI-powered enrichment, waterfall workflows, no-code integrations, credit-based consolidation

Migration Notes

Clearbit → Clay

Effort: Medium (1-2 weeks)

Export enriched contact data from HubSpot/Clearbit as CSV and import into Clay. The main work is rebuilding automatic enrichment workflows in Clay's table-based UI. Expect to spend time mapping Clearbit fields to Clay columns and configuring waterfall providers.

What you gain: 50+ data sources, AI research agent, CRM-agnostic flexibility

What you lose: Zero-config HubSpot enrichment, Reveal IP-to-company, form shortening

Clay → Clearbit (HubSpot)

Effort: Low (2-3 days)

If you're already on HubSpot, enabling Breeze Intelligence is straightforward. Export Clay enriched data to HubSpot, then configure Breeze credits for ongoing enrichment. The transition is easy but you'll lose multi-source depth.

What you gain: Automatic HubSpot enrichment, Reveal, form shortening, enterprise compliance

What you lose: Waterfall enrichment across 50+ providers, Claygent AI, workflow flexibility

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep or marketer: Clearbit (Breeze Intelligence) is easier if you're already in HubSpot. It works in the background with no setup. Clay requires more effort but rewards you with deeper, more accurate data.

If you're a sales leader or RevOps team: Clay's Org score of 73 vs Clearbit's 65 reflects its broader capabilities. If data quality across multiple sources matters more than CRM convenience, Clay wins. If you're standardized on HubSpot and want one vendor for everything, Clearbit/Breeze keeps the stack simple.

The bigger picture: Clearbit as a standalone product is fading. If you're evaluating enrichment tools today, Clay is the more future-proof choice unless you're deeply committed to the HubSpot ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Clearbit still available as a standalone product?

Clearbit was acquired by HubSpot in 2023 and is being absorbed into HubSpot Breeze Intelligence. While legacy Clearbit accounts may still exist, new customers are directed to Breeze Intelligence credits within the HubSpot ecosystem. Standalone Clearbit is effectively being sunset.

Can Clay replace Clearbit for data enrichment?

Yes. Clay's waterfall enrichment across 50+ data providers typically delivers higher match rates than Clearbit's single-source approach. However, if you're a HubSpot-native team, Breeze Intelligence offers tighter CRM integration with less setup. Clay requires more configuration but offers far more flexibility.

Which tool is better for HubSpot users?

Clearbit (Breeze Intelligence) offers native HubSpot integration with zero setup. Enrichment happens automatically on form submissions and CRM records. Clay connects to HubSpot but requires building enrichment workflows manually. For pure convenience, Breeze wins. For data quality and depth, Clay wins.
