Migration Notes

ZoomInfo → Cognism

Effort: Medium (1-2 weeks)

Export contact lists from ZoomInfo and import into Cognism. Rebuild saved searches and list criteria using Cognism's filters. The main adjustment is adapting to a smaller US database while gaining stronger EMEA coverage and GDPR compliance. CRM integrations transfer smoothly.

What you gain: GDPR compliance, EU data quality, phone-verified mobiles, better vendor practices

What you lose: Largest US database, built-in intent signals, full platform breadth (marketing, recruiting)

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep: Cognism's Rep score of 58 vs ZoomInfo's 51 reflects a better individual user experience. Cognism's Chrome extension and Diamond Data mobiles give reps more direct value day-to-day. ZoomInfo's Rep score is lower due to its organizational complexity and credit-based usage model that can feel restrictive for individual users.

If you're a sales leader: ZoomInfo's Org score of 77 vs Cognism's 72 reflects its platform breadth, database size, and enterprise scalability. If your team is primarily US-focused and needs the largest database with built-in intent, ZoomInfo is the industry standard. If you sell into EMEA, GDPR compliance matters, or phone-verified mobiles are critical, Cognism is the clear choice and typically costs less.

Geography is the key factor: For US-focused teams, ZoomInfo. For EMEA-focused teams, Cognism. For global teams, consider using both or choosing based on where the majority of your revenue comes from.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cognism or ZoomInfo better for European prospecting?

Cognism is the clear winner for European prospecting. It was built as an EU-first platform with GDPR compliance at its core, phone-verified mobile numbers for EMEA markets, and EU data residency. ZoomInfo's European data depth doesn't match its North American coverage, and its GDPR compliance approach has been less central to its product design.

Can I use Cognism and ZoomInfo together?

Yes, and some enterprise teams do exactly this. They use ZoomInfo for North American prospecting where it excels, and Cognism for EMEA territories where it has stronger data and compliance. However, this doubles your data vendor costs, so it only makes sense for companies with significant revenue in both regions.

Which tool has better mobile phone data?

Cognism has an edge on phone-verified mobile numbers, especially in EMEA. Its Diamond Data feature provides human-verified mobiles with 98% accuracy claims. ZoomInfo has a larger overall database but mobile verification rates vary. For teams where phone outreach is critical, Cognism's verified mobiles are a key differentiator.
