Migration Notes

Lusha → Apollo

Effort: Low-Medium (2-4 days)

Export contacts from Lusha and import into Apollo. The main work is setting up Apollo's email sequences and workflows, which are more complex than Lusha's simple lookup model. Teams gain outreach capabilities but need training on the broader platform.

What you gain: All-in-one platform, email sequences, dialer, 275M+ database, free tier

What you lose: Simplest UX, direct dial accuracy advantage, zero-learning-curve lookups

Apollo → Lusha

Effort: Low (1-2 days)

Export contacts from Apollo and import into your CRM. Install Lusha's Chrome extension. The transition is quick, but you'll need to source separate tools for email sequences and calling that Apollo provided natively.

What you gain: Simpler UX, better direct dials, EU compliance

What you lose: Built-in sequences, dialer, 275M+ database, free tier volume

The Verdict

If you're an individual rep: Apollo's free tier (10K leads/mo + sequences + dialer) provides more value than Lusha's paid Pro plan. However, if you just need to quickly look up a phone number and call, Lusha's simplicity is unmatched. Rep scores are close: Apollo 72, Lusha 70.

If you're a sales leader: Apollo's Org score of 68 vs Lusha's 60 reflects its broader platform value. For teams, Apollo eliminates the need for separate outreach tools, reducing total cost of ownership. Lusha only wins if your team already has outreach tools and just needs a focused contact data layer.

If EU compliance is critical: Both support EU data residency, but Lusha's EU focus is slightly stronger. Apollo also offers EU hosting, so this is not a differentiator for most teams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lusha or Apollo better value for money?

Apollo offers significantly more value per dollar as an all-in-one platform. Its free tier alone provides 10K leads/mo, built-in email sequences, and a dialer. Lusha is simpler and cheaper for basic contact lookups, but you'll need separate tools for outreach. For total cost of ownership, Apollo typically wins.

Which has better direct dial phone numbers?

Lusha is generally stronger for direct dial phone numbers, especially in European markets. Apollo has a larger overall database (275M+ contacts) but users report that Lusha's phone number accuracy is higher for individual direct dials. If phone outreach is your primary channel, Lusha has the edge.

Can Lusha replace Apollo for sales prospecting?

Lusha cannot fully replace Apollo because it lacks built-in email sequences, a phone dialer, and the 275M+ contact database. Lusha is a focused contact data tool, while Apollo is an all-in-one sales platform. If you switch from Apollo to Lusha, you'll need separate tools for email outreach and calling.
