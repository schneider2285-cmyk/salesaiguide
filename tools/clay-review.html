What Is Clay?

Clay is a data enrichment and prospecting platform that lets you access 50+ data providers (Apollo, ZoomInfo, Hunter, Clearbit, etc.) from one spreadsheet interface. Instead of paying for multiple data tools separately, Clay gives you credits that work across all providers. The real power is the AI layer on top — use ChatGPT/Claude to analyze prospect data, write personalized emails, and automate research at scale.

Key Features

50+ Data Providers in One

Access Apollo, ZoomInfo, Hunter, Clearbit, and 40+ other providers from a single interface using unified credits. Different providers excel in different areas — Clay gives you all of them without separate contracts.

AI-Powered Enrichment

Use ChatGPT or Claude directly inside Clay columns to research companies, write personalized opening lines, and score prospect fit against your ICP — all automatically for every row.

Waterfall Enrichment

Set up fallback sequences so if one provider doesn't have a result, Clay queries the next automatically. In testing, waterfall sequences hit 90%+ email match rates vs. 60-70% from single providers. (source)

No-Code Integrations

Connects to 200+ tools including HubSpot, Salesforce, Instantly, Lemlist, Slack, and Google Sheets. Build end-to-end workflows without writing code.

Spreadsheet Interface

Familiar spreadsheet layout that sales reps can use on day one. Each column can execute live API calls, run AI prompts, and trigger enrichment workflows.

Team Collaboration

Shared workspace with reusable workflow templates. Team members can create standard ICP qualification flows the entire sales org can apply to new prospect lists.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Replaces 5+ tools — cancelled Apollo, ZoomInfo, and Hunter after switching
  • Waterfall enrichment means you get best data from whichever source has it
  • AI integration is game-changing — company research at scale in seconds
  • Saves hours — 30-min manual research now takes 2 minutes with automation
  • Active Slack community and excellent template library

What Could Be Better

  • Steep 1-2 week learning curve before you're comfortable
  • Credit costs add up fast if you're not careful with enrichment flows
  • Can feel overwhelming with so many features
  • No built-in email sending — need Instantly or Lemlist separately

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • Enterprise AEs doing personalized, high-value outreach to VP+ buyers
  • Companies targeting enterprise accounts where each deal needs deep research
  • RevOps teams building automated enrichment pipelines into CRM and outreach tools
  • Teams willing to invest time learning for outsized return on data quality

Skip If You

  • Teams needing simple, plug-and-play solution with minimal setup
  • Teams doing high-volume SMB outreach (Apollo is cheaper with built-in sequences)
  • Teams without $149+/month budget
  • Anyone who wants built-in email sending

Limitations & Watch-Outs

No tool is perfect. Here are the downsides we found during testing.

  • Credit costs can escalate quickly without careful monitoring of enrichment workflows
  • Steep learning curve requires 1-2 weeks before comfortable with the platform
  • No built-in email sending capabilities — requires pairing with Instantly or Lemlist
  • Complex UI can overwhelm teams that just need simple prospecting

When to Choose Clay: Use Cases

Clay works best in specific scenarios where its strengths align with your team's needs.

  • Enterprise account-based outreach requiring deep prospect research
  • RevOps teams building automated data enrichment pipelines
  • Agencies managing prospecting across multiple client accounts
  • Teams replacing multiple point tools with a unified data platform
