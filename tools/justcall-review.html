What Is JustCall?

JustCall is a cloud-based phone system built for sales and support teams that need outbound calling, SMS messaging, and CRM integration in a single platform. The company launched in 2016 and has focused on serving small to mid-size businesses that want dialer functionality without the complexity or cost of enterprise telephony platforms like Five9 or Genesys. Where dedicated parallel dialers like Orum optimize for maximum simultaneous connections across large SDR teams, JustCall targets the broader market of teams that need reliable calling infrastructure with SMS built in from the start rather than bolted on as an add-on.

The platform covers three core workflows: power dialing for sequential outbound calling, bulk SMS campaigns for text-based follow-up, and call recording with automatic CRM logging. JustCall connects natively to Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and over a hundred other tools through direct integrations and Zapier, with call activity and SMS conversation data flowing back to contact records automatically so pipeline reporting stays accurate without manual logging. For teams where phone outreach is a primary channel but the budget does not support a dedicated contact center platform, JustCall occupies a practical middle ground between basic VoIP and full-featured sales dialers. The combination of calling and texting in one interface matters because inside sales workflows increasingly blend both channels, and managing them through separate tools creates data silos that fragment prospect engagement history across disconnected systems.

Key Features We Tested

✓ Power Dialer

Automates sequential dialing from an imported contact list. The dialer moves to the next number automatically after each call ends, logging the outcome to your connected CRM. Reps stay on the phone instead of manually searching for and dialing the next number.

✓ SMS Campaigns

Send bulk text messages to segmented contact lists with templates and scheduling. SMS open rates consistently outperform email in sales workflows, and JustCall handles both individual and campaign-level texting from the same interface used for calling.

✓ Call Recording

Records all inbound and outbound calls automatically with storage accessible from the dashboard and synced to CRM records. Managers can review calls for coaching without asking reps to save or forward recordings manually.

✓ CRM Integration

Native two-way sync with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive logs calls, SMS messages, and voicemails directly to contact records. Activity data flows without manual entry, keeping pipeline reporting accurate as call volume scales.

✓ Local Presence Dialing

Displays a local area code matching the prospect's region when placing outbound calls. Local numbers consistently increase answer rates compared to toll-free or out-of-area codes, which matters for teams calling across multiple geographies.

✓ Voicemail Drop

Pre-record voicemail messages and drop them with a single click when a call goes to voicemail. This feature saves thirty to sixty seconds per unanswered call, which compounds across a full day of dialing into meaningful time recovered for live conversations.

What JustCall Gets Right (and Wrong)

After testing JustCall across power dialing, SMS campaign, and CRM integration workflows over multiple weeks of daily use, here is an honest breakdown of where the platform delivers and where it falls short. These findings come from hands-on outbound calling with real prospect lists and CRM sync verification, not from marketing materials or feature documentation alone. We evaluated JustCall against the criteria that matter most to small sales teams: call quality, CRM data accuracy, SMS delivery rates, and total cost of ownership including per-minute charges.

✓ What Works Well

  • Calling plus SMS in one platform: Most competing dialers treat SMS as an afterthought or charge extra. JustCall builds text messaging into the same workflow as calling, which simplifies the outbound stack for teams that use both channels
  • Affordable entry point: Starting at nineteen dollars per user per month, JustCall undercuts most dedicated sales dialers while including call recording, CRM sync, and basic analytics in the base plan
  • Clean CRM sync: The Salesforce and HubSpot integrations logged calls and SMS activity reliably during testing without requiring manual intervention or duplicate record cleanup
  • Fast onboarding: Number provisioning and CRM connection take under thirty minutes. The power dialer interface is intuitive enough that reps can start calling on the same day without dedicated training

✗ Key Limitations

  • No parallel dialing: JustCall is a sequential power dialer only. Teams that need to call multiple numbers simultaneously and route live connections to available reps will need a dedicated parallel dialer like Orum
  • AI features are basic: Call transcription and sentiment analysis exist on higher-tier plans but lack the depth of Dialpad's real-time coaching engine or dedicated conversation intelligence tools like Gong
  • Per-minute charges add up: Lower-tier plans include limited calling minutes with per-minute overage charges that vary by destination country. High-volume teams should model total monthly cost carefully beyond the base subscription price
  • Reporting lacks granularity: Call analytics cover volume, duration, and outcomes at the team level but do not provide the sequence-step attribution or A/B testing insights found in more mature sales engagement platforms

When to Choose JustCall: Use Cases

✓ Right Fit For:

  • Small sales teams with two to fifteen reps where phone and SMS are primary outbound channels and the budget favors practical tools over enterprise platforms
  • Inside sales teams that combine call blitzes with text message follow-up and need both channels managed from a single interface with shared contact records
  • Startups and SMBs that need CRM-connected calling infrastructure deployed in a day without IT support or telephony expertise on staff
  • Teams currently using personal phones or basic VoIP that want call recording, local presence dialing, and activity logging added to their sales workflow

✗ Not the Right Tool If:

  • You need parallel dialing to maximize live connections per hour across a large SDR team doing two hundred or more dials daily
  • You want real-time AI coaching that surfaces objection handling prompts and talk-to-listen ratios during live calls
  • Your calling volume exceeds the included minutes and per-minute overage charges make the total cost uncompetitive versus unlimited-minute platforms
  • You require advanced call analytics with sequence-step attribution, conversion tracking, and manager dashboards beyond basic volume and duration metrics

Final Verdict

★★★★☆
4.0/5 — SAG editorial score

JustCall is a practical choice for small sales teams that need calling and SMS in one platform at an accessible price point.

Its power dialer, bulk SMS, and native CRM integrations cover the core workflows that inside sales teams use daily without the complexity or cost of enterprise telephony. The trade-off is clear: JustCall handles sequential calling and text outreach well but does not attempt parallel dialing, real-time AI coaching, or deep conversation analytics. For teams under fifteen reps where phone and SMS are primary channels and the budget matters, JustCall delivers reliable infrastructure without overbuilding. The per-minute overage pricing on lower tiers is the main cost trap to watch — teams doing high daily call volumes should model total monthly spend including overages before committing to a plan. Teams that outgrow its feature set will naturally move toward Dialpad for AI capabilities or Orum for parallel dialing volume.
