What Is JustCall?

JustCall is a cloud phone system positioned as the most affordable full-featured option for small and mid-size sales teams. It provides VoIP calling, SMS, power dialer, and CRM integrations at price points below Aircall and Kixie while covering the core functionality most inside sales teams need.

The platform is designed for inside sales teams that make a high volume of outbound calls and want to combine phone outreach with SMS follow-ups in a single workflow. JustCall handles the infrastructure — local and toll-free numbers, call routing, and recording — so teams can start dialing without involving IT or managing their own telephony stack. For growing sales organizations that need a phone system today but cannot commit to enterprise contract pricing, JustCall fills a practical gap in the market.

Setup is straightforward: you sign up, choose a local or toll-free phone number, connect your CRM, and start making calls from the browser-based dialer or mobile app. Most teams are operational within a single afternoon. JustCall provisions numbers in over 70 countries, which is useful for teams prospecting international markets that want to show a local caller ID rather than an unfamiliar international number. The platform also supports number porting, so teams switching from another provider can keep their existing business numbers.

One area where JustCall differentiates from more expensive competitors is its approach to SMS as a first-class outreach channel alongside voice. Many sales dialers treat SMS as an afterthought, but JustCall builds SMS templates, automation sequences, and shared inbox management directly into the platform. For teams where prospects respond better to text messages than phone calls — common in industries like real estate, insurance, and field services — this integrated SMS capability is a meaningful advantage over voice-only tools.

The mobile app deserves a mention because it makes JustCall genuinely usable for reps who split time between the office and the field. Calls and texts made from the mobile app still use your JustCall business number rather than the rep's personal cell, which keeps the professional boundary intact and ensures all activity is logged in the CRM. For field sales teams or remote reps who take calls from various locations throughout the day, this is a practical detail that some competing platforms handle less gracefully.

JustCall also offers a shared phone number feature where multiple reps can handle calls and SMS threads on the same business line. This is useful for teams where leads are distributed among several reps or where a general sales line needs to be staffed during business hours. Incoming calls can be routed based on rules — round-robin distribution, skills-based routing, or time-of-day rules — giving small teams some of the call routing sophistication that typically comes with more expensive contact center platforms.

From an administration perspective, JustCall provides a centralized dashboard where managers can view call logs, listen to recordings, and monitor real-time activity across the team. The permission system lets admins control which reps can access recordings, export data, or modify account settings. For teams with compliance requirements around call recording — common in financial services and insurance — the ability to set automatic recording policies and retention rules at the account level is important.

Key Features

VoIP Calling

Cloud phone system with local and toll-free numbers in 70+ countries. Includes call forwarding, IVR menus, call queues, and voicemail. Both desktop and mobile apps are available, so reps can take calls from their laptop at the office or their phone on the road without switching tools.

SMS Automation

Two-way SMS with automated follow-up sequences, pre-built templates, and bulk messaging capabilities. The shared SMS inbox lets multiple team members collaborate on prospect conversations, which is helpful when handoffs happen between SDRs and account executives mid-thread.

Auto Dialer

Power dialer for working through call lists efficiently with minimal downtime between connections. Includes voicemail drop for pre-recorded messages, call outcome logging so reps can tag results without leaving the dialer, and CRM record display that shows prospect context before each call connects.

CRM Integrations

Native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and 100+ other tools via direct connectors and Zapier. Calls and SMS are auto-logged to CRM records, which eliminates the manual activity logging that reps typically skip at the end of a busy day.

Call Recording & Analytics

Automatic call recording with a team analytics dashboard that tracks call volume, average talk time, and conversion metrics by individual rep. Managers can filter by date range, team, or rep to identify patterns in outbound activity and connect calling behavior to outcomes.

AI Call Scoring

AI-powered call quality scoring and sentiment analysis available on the Pro plan. The system evaluates calls for tone, key phrases, and conversation flow to surface coaching opportunities without requiring managers to listen to every recording individually.

Pricing Breakdown

JustCall uses per-seat pricing with monthly and annual billing options. The Essentials plan covers core calling and basic SMS, while the Team plan adds the power dialer, SMS automation sequences, and advanced analytics that most sales teams need for productive outbound workflows. Annual billing offers a discount compared to monthly pricing, which is worth considering for teams that have committed to the platform after their trial period.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Most affordable full-featured sales dialer in its category
  • 100+ CRM integrations is the broadest selection in this price range
  • SMS automation alongside calling is well-integrated in a single platform
  • Quick setup with no IT involvement required — most teams are live within a few hours
  • International number provisioning across 70+ countries simplifies global outbound campaigns

What Could Be Better

  • UI and UX less polished than Aircall — the interface works but feels dated in places
  • Call quality occasionally reported as lower than premium dialers, particularly on international routes
  • AI features less sophisticated than Dialpad for conversation analysis and coaching
  • Support responsiveness can be slow, especially on the Essentials plan without priority access
  • Reporting dashboards lack customization options that larger teams expect for tracking complex workflows

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • Small and mid-size businesses that need a full-featured sales dialer at the lowest possible price point
  • Teams on CRMs beyond the big 3 (JustCall's 100+ integrations cover niche tools)
  • Teams that want SMS automation as part of their outreach toolkit
  • Remote sales teams that need a cloud phone system their reps can use from any device without hardware setup

Skip If You

  • Large enterprise teams that need enterprise-grade call quality, reliability guarantees, and dedicated support SLAs
  • Teams where call quality perception is critical to overall brand image and reputation
  • Organizations that need advanced conversation intelligence features like deal scoring or call coaching at the level of Gong or Dialpad

Limitations & Watch-Outs

No tool is perfect. Here are the downsides we found during testing.

  • Call quality can be inconsistent in some geographic regions
  • Advanced features locked behind higher-tier plans
  • Limited analytics compared to dedicated conversation intelligence tools
  • SMS capabilities vary by country and carrier restrictions

When to Choose JustCall: Use Cases

JustCall works best in specific scenarios where its strengths align with your team's needs.

  • Inside sales teams needing a cloud phone system with CRM sync
  • Support teams handling inbound and outbound calls at scale
  • Managers needing call recording and performance analytics
  • Teams requiring SMS outreach alongside voice communication
