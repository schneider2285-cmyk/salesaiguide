What Is JustCall?

JustCall is a cloud phone system positioned as the most affordable full-featured option for small and mid-size sales teams. It provides VoIP calling, SMS, power dialer, and CRM integrations at price points below Aircall and Kixie while covering the core functionality most inside sales teams need.

Key Features

VoIP Calling

Cloud phone system with numbers in 70+ countries. Call forwarding, IVR, queues, and voicemail. Desktop and mobile apps.

SMS Automation

Two-way SMS with automated follow-up sequences, templates, and bulk messaging. SMS inbox management with team collaboration.

Auto Dialer

Power dialer for working through call lists efficiently. Voicemail drop, call outcome logging, and CRM record display.

CRM Integrations

Native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and 100+ other tools. Calls and SMS auto-logged to CRM records.

Call Recording & Analytics

Automatic recording with team analytics dashboard. Call volume, talk time, and conversion metrics by rep.

AI Call Scoring

AI-powered call quality scoring and sentiment analysis on higher plans. Identify coaching opportunities without listening to every call.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Most affordable full-featured sales dialer — 30-40% less than Aircall/Kixie
  • 100+ CRM integrations is broadest in this category
  • SMS automation alongside calling is well-integrated
  • Quick setup with no IT requirements

What Could Be Better

  • UI and UX less polished than Aircall
  • Call quality occasionally reported as lower than premium dialers
  • AI features less sophisticated than Dialpad
  • Support responsiveness can be slow

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • SMBs that need a full-featured sales dialer at the lowest possible price
  • Teams on CRMs beyond the big 3 (JustCall's 100+ integrations cover niche tools)
  • Teams that want SMS automation as part of their outreach toolkit

Skip If You

  • Large enterprise teams that need enterprise-grade reliability and support SLAs
  • Teams where call quality perception is critical to brand image
