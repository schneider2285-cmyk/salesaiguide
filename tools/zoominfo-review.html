What Is ZoomInfo?

ZoomInfo is the dominant enterprise B2B data platform, providing access to 100M+ business profiles with verified emails, direct phone numbers, org charts, intent data, and website visitor identification. It serves as the data backbone for enterprise sales and marketing teams running account-based strategies.

Beyond raw contact data, ZoomInfo includes buyer intent signals, website visitor tracking (via its Chorus acquisition), workflow automation, and a data enrichment API. The platform has expanded through acquisitions of Chorus, RingLead, and other companies to become a comprehensive revenue operations platform.

Note: This profile is based on vendor documentation and G2 reviews, not firsthand testing. For user reviews, see ZoomInfo on G2.

Pricing Breakdown

TL;DR: No public pricing. Annual contracts typically start at $15K–25K/year. Mid-size teams commonly pay $50K–100K/year. Enterprise deals can exceed $200K/year with all modules.

⚠ Pricing Gotchas

  • Annual contracts only with significant minimums ($15K+/year) — no monthly billing available
  • Credit-based system where different actions (contact export, enrichment, intent) cost different credit amounts
  • Separate modules for intent data, website visitor tracking, and enrichment — each adds cost
  • Overage charges if you exceed your credit allotment mid-contract
  • Multi-year lock-in discounts create significant switching costs and reduce negotiation leverage at renewal

What ZoomInfo Is Great At

  • Highest-quality B2B data: Best-in-market data accuracy for North American contacts with verified emails and direct dials
  • Intent data signals: Identify companies actively researching your product category before competitors reach them
  • Org chart mapping: Visualize buying committees, reporting structures, and decision-makers within target accounts
  • Enterprise compliance: SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and CCPA compliant with dedicated security and privacy infrastructure
  • Website visitor identification: De-anonymize website traffic and connect visitors to company profiles and contacts
  • Deepest integration ecosystem: Native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Salesloft, and 50+ enterprise sales tools

Where ZoomInfo Breaks at Scale

  • Extremely expensive: Easily $50K–100K/year for mid-size teams; enterprise deals can exceed $200K/year with all modules
  • International data inconsistency: Data quality for European, APAC, and emerging markets is significantly weaker than North America
  • Opaque pricing creates procurement headaches: No public pricing means lengthy sales cycles and difficult budget planning
  • Platform complexity from acquisitions: Chorus, RingLead, and other acquired products can feel disconnected from the core platform
  • Credit consumption is hard to predict: Different actions burn credits at different rates, making cost forecasting difficult
  • Smaller company coverage gaps: Startups and sub-50-employee companies often have incomplete or outdated profiles

Who Should NOT Buy ZoomInfo

  • Startups and SMBs: Apollo.io delivers 80% of the value at 1/10th the cost — use that until your budget supports ZoomInfo
  • Teams focused on European markets: Cognism has better EMEA data quality and GDPR-native compliance
  • Companies needing just email verification: Hunter.io or Bouncer does email verification at a fraction of ZoomInfo's cost
  • Solo SDRs or small teams under 10: The ROI at $15K+/year doesn't justify the cost for small teams
  • Organizations wanting transparent pricing: If opaque contracts and lengthy sales processes frustrate you, look at Apollo or Lusha

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does ZoomInfo cost for small companies?

ZoomInfo does not offer pricing for small companies in the traditional sense. Annual contracts typically start at $15K–25K/year with credit-based access. For startups and SMBs, Apollo.io offers comparable (though smaller) data at 1/10th the cost starting at $49/month.

How does ZoomInfo compare to Apollo.io?

ZoomInfo has higher data quality for North American enterprise contacts and includes intent data, org charts, and website visitor tracking. Apollo.io is an all-in-one platform with built-in email sending and CRM at a fraction of the cost. Choose ZoomInfo for premium enterprise data; choose Apollo for all-in-one prospecting on a budget.

How accurate is ZoomInfo data?

ZoomInfo is generally considered the most accurate B2B data provider for North American contacts, with reported accuracy rates of 85–95% for emails and 70–85% for direct phone numbers. However, data quality drops significantly for international markets, smaller companies, and rapidly changing industries.

How good is ZoomInfo's international data?

ZoomInfo's strength is North American data. International coverage, particularly for European markets, is inconsistent compared to specialists like Cognism. If your primary market is EMEA, Cognism is generally a better choice for contact data quality and GDPR compliance.

How can I negotiate a better ZoomInfo contract?

Common negotiation tactics include getting quotes from Apollo or Cognism as leverage, negotiating at end-of-quarter when reps have quotas, pushing back on multi-year lock-ins, requesting credit rollover provisions, and asking for module bundling discounts. Be prepared to walk away — ZoomInfo's sales team is trained to hold pricing.
