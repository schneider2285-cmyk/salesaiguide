What Is ZoomInfo?

ZoomInfo is the dominant enterprise B2B data platform, providing 100M+ business profiles with verified emails, direct dials, org charts, intent data, and website visitor identification. It has expanded through acquisitions (Chorus, RingLead) into a comprehensive revenue operations platform with enrichment APIs and workflow automation. Profile based on vendor docs and G2 reviews. See ZoomInfo on G2.

Key Features

100M+ B2B Contact Profiles

Verified emails, direct dials, and company data for 100M+ professionals.

Intent Data & Buyer Signals

Identify companies researching your product via web activity signals.

Website Visitor Identification

De-anonymize site visitors and match them to buying committees.

Org Chart & Buying Committees

Map org hierarchies and identify decision-makers in target accounts.

Real-time API to enrich CRM records and leads from ZoomInfo's database.

Workflow Automation

Trigger CRM updates, sequences, and routing based on data signals.

Quick Verdict

✓ Best For

Enterprise teams (50+ reps) targeting North American B2B companies needing top-tier contact data, intent signals, and org charts.

✗ Avoid If

You're a startup or SMB, your primary market is Europe, or you need transparent pricing and flexible contracts.

Bottom Line

ZoomInfo is the gold standard for enterprise B2B data — unmatched data quality for North American contacts, but the price tag ($15K–100K+/year), opaque pricing, and complex contracts make it a significant investment that only makes sense for well-funded teams.

Pricing Breakdown

TL;DR: No public pricing; starts ~$15K/year, mid-size $50K–100K, enterprise $200K+.

⚠ Pricing Gotchas

  • Annual contracts only, $15K+ minimum — no monthly billing
  • Credit-based system — different actions burn credits at different rates
  • Intent data, visitor tracking, and enrichment are separate paid modules
  • Overage charges if you exceed credit allotment mid-contract
  • Multi-year lock-ins reduce negotiation leverage at renewal

What ZoomInfo Is Great At

  • Highest-quality B2B data: Best accuracy for North American contacts with verified emails and dials
  • Intent data signals: Identify in-market companies before competitors reach them
  • Org chart mapping: Visualize buying committees and decision-makers in target accounts
  • Website visitor identification: De-anonymize traffic and connect visitors to contacts
  • Deepest integration ecosystem: 50+ native integrations with major sales tools

Where ZoomInfo Breaks at Scale

  • Extremely expensive: $50K–100K/year mid-size; $200K+ enterprise
  • International data gaps: EMEA/APAC data significantly weaker than North America
  • Opaque pricing: No public pricing; lengthy sales cycles
  • Acquisition complexity: Acquired products can feel disconnected
  • Unpredictable credit burn: Actions consume credits at varying rates
  • Small company gaps: Sub-50-employee companies often incomplete

Who Should NOT Buy ZoomInfo

  • Startups and SMBs: Apollo.io delivers 80% of the value at 1/10th cost
  • EMEA-focused teams: Cognism has better European data
  • Email verification only: Hunter.io or Bouncer cost far less
  • Solo SDRs or teams under 10: $15K+/year rarely justified
  • Transparent pricing seekers: Try Apollo or Lusha instead

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does ZoomInfo cost for small companies?

Annual contracts start at $15K–25K/year. For startups, Apollo.io offers comparable data from $49/month.

How does ZoomInfo compare to Apollo.io?

ZoomInfo has superior North American data plus intent signals. Apollo.io is all-in-one at a fraction of the cost. Choose ZoomInfo for enterprise data; Apollo for budget prospecting.

How accurate is ZoomInfo data?

Most accurate B2B data for North American contacts (85–95% email, 70–85% direct dial). Quality drops internationally.
