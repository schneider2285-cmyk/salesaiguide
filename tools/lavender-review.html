What Is Lavender?

Lavender is an AI email coaching Chrome extension that scores cold emails in real-time and provides specific suggestions to improve reply rates. Unlike tools that write for you, Lavender coaches you — including DISC-based prospect personality analysis and team coaching dashboards for managers. Profile based on vendor docs and G2 reviews. See Lavender on G2.

Key Features

Real-Time Email Scoring

Scores every email as you write with coaching to improve replies.

AI Rewrite Suggestions

One-click rewrites that shorten and strengthen email copy.

Personality Analysis (DISC)

Identifies prospect communication style and suggests tone adjustments.

Email Template Analysis

Score existing templates to identify top performers and why.

Team Coaching Dashboards

Manager analytics showing team email quality and coaching gaps.

Chrome Extension Integration

Works inside Gmail, Outlook, Outreach, and Salesloft natively.

Quick Verdict

✓ Best For

SDRs and AEs sending cold email daily who want real-time coaching to permanently improve writing and reply rates.

✗ Avoid If

You need a sending platform (Lavender only coaches, doesn't send) or your team doesn't send cold email regularly.

Bottom Line

Lavender is the best-in-class email coaching tool — AI is the entire product, not an add-on. The trade-off is that it only helps with email, not calls, LinkedIn, or other channels.
