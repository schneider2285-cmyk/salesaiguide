What Is Lavender?

Lavender is an AI email writing assistant and coach that sits inside Gmail and Outlook. It analyzes your email in real time as you write, scoring it against patterns that correlate with high reply rates and providing specific suggestions to improve subject lines, email length, personalization, and mobile readability. Unlike Instantly or Lemlist which are sending platforms, Lavender is purely a writing and coaching tool.

Key Features

Real-Time Email Scoring

Scores your email 0-100 as you write it, updating in real time based on length, reading level, personalization, spam trigger words, and mobile-friendliness. Shows exactly what's hurting your score.

AI Writing Coach

GPT-powered suggestions for improving subject lines, opening lines, and call-to-action. Generates alternative versions of weak phrases and identifies generic language that should be personalized.

Personalization Assistant

Pulls LinkedIn data, news mentions, and company updates to suggest personalized icebreakers and value props based on the specific prospect you're emailing.

Inbox Integration

Chrome extension that works directly inside Gmail, Outlook, Salesloft, Outreach, and other email tools. No context-switching — coaching appears in the tool you already use.

Manager Dashboards

Team analytics showing average email scores by rep, A/B testing results, and coaching recommendations per team member. Identify which reps need the most help.

Templates

Pre-built high-performing email templates for cold outreach, follow-ups, and meeting requests. Customizable with your value props and ICP details.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Real-time email scoring genuinely improves email quality with specific feedback
  • Works directly inside Gmail and Outlook — no context switching
  • AI personalization assistant saves research time per email
  • Team analytics give managers objective coaching data
  • $29/month is low friction for individual rep productivity improvement

What Could Be Better

  • Only helps with email writing — not a sending or sequencing platform
  • Personalization suggestions can be generic without deep ICP customization
  • Free tier limited to 5 emails/day — not useful for high-volume SDRs
  • AI suggestions occasionally produce formal, robotic copy

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • SDRs sending 20+ cold emails per day who want measurable reply rate improvement
  • Sales managers who want objective email quality data for coaching conversations
  • Teams already using Gmail + Salesloft or Outreach who want better email quality
  • Anyone who feels their email copy could be better but isn't sure why

Skip If You

  • Teams primarily using phone calls as main outreach (Lavender is email-only)
  • Managers who need a sending or sequencing platform (use Instantly or Lemlist)
  • Reps already consistently hitting 15%+ reply rates on their own

Limitations & Watch-Outs

No tool is perfect. Here are the downsides we found during testing.

  • Only useful if your team sends significant email volume
  • Limited value for teams using phone-first outreach strategies
  • AI suggestions require human judgment — not always contextually perfect
  • Pricing per seat can add up for larger teams
