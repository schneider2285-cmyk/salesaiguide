What Is Clearbit?

Clearbit is a B2B data enrichment platform acquired by HubSpot in 2023. It provides real-time company and contact data enrichment, website visitor identification, and form shortening for HubSpot customers. Its deepest value is for organizations running HubSpot-centric go-to-market motions where enriched contact and company data flows automatically into marketing automation and sales workflows.

Key Features

HubSpot-Native Sync

Real-time bidirectional data sync with HubSpot CRM. Automatically enriches new contacts with company data, job titles, and firmographics as they enter your HubSpot database.

Form Shortening

Reduce form fields by auto-filling known data for returning visitors. Instead of asking for company size and industry, Clearbit fills those in automatically, improving form conversion rates.

Company Reveal

Identify the company behind anonymous website visits. Shows which organizations are browsing your site even if visitors don't fill out a form — prioritize outreach to engaged accounts.

Contact Enrichment

Enrich contact records with job title, seniority, department, and LinkedIn URL. Company enrichment adds employee count, revenue, tech stack, and funding data.

Prospector

Search Clearbit's database by company characteristics and job title to build targeted prospect lists. Export to HubSpot, CSV, or directly to sales tools.

Traffic Rank & Intent

Company traffic rank and buying intent signals help prioritize which accounts to pursue based on digital footprint and topic research patterns.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • HubSpot-native integration is genuinely seamless — no API configuration needed
  • Form shortening demonstrably improves lead form conversion rates
  • Website visitor reveal is unique and actionable for marketing teams
  • Real-time enrichment keeps CRM data fresh automatically
  • Strong brand recognition with GTM/marketing team buyers

What Could Be Better

  • Expensive for what you get compared to Clay's multi-provider approach
  • No transparent pricing — requires sales engagement
  • Best value only for HubSpot-centric organizations
  • Less useful for purely outbound sales motions without inbound context

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • HubSpot Marketing Hub customers who want automatic contact enrichment in their CRM
  • Marketing teams that want form shortening and website visitor identification
  • Revenue operations teams building HubSpot-centric data workflows

Skip If You

  • Salesforce-centric organizations (ZoomInfo or Clay are better fits)
  • Purely outbound sales teams without inbound marketing motion
  • Teams that want enrichment from 50+ providers (use Clay instead)

Limitations & Watch-Outs

No tool is perfect. Here are the downsides we found during testing.

  • Now integrated into HubSpot — standalone product being phased out
  • Enrichment accuracy decreases for smaller companies and startups
  • Pricing not transparent — requires sales call for enterprise features
  • Limited value outside the HubSpot ecosystem going forward

When to Choose Clearbit: Use Cases

Clearbit works best in specific scenarios where its strengths align with your team's needs.

  • HubSpot-native teams needing real-time lead enrichment
  • Marketing teams scoring and routing inbound leads automatically
  • Companies wanting enriched form fills to reduce friction
  • RevOps teams building automated lead qualification workflows
