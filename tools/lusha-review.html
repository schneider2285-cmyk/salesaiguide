What Is Lusha?

Lusha is a contact data platform specializing in direct dial phone numbers and verified email addresses. Where Apollo.io offers a full prospecting suite and Clay connects dozens of providers, Lusha focuses on doing one thing very well: finding direct dials and emails quickly with a Chrome extension that works on LinkedIn and company websites. It's the tool reps reach for when they need a phone number fast.

Key Features

Direct Dial Numbers

High accuracy direct dial phone numbers for decision-makers. Lusha's direct dial data is among the most accurate in the market, with regularly refreshed coverage for US and EU markets.

Email Finder

Verified business email addresses matched to contact and company records. Bulk email finding via CSV or list import for enriching larger prospect lists.

Chrome Extension

One-click contact lookup on LinkedIn profiles, Sales Navigator, and company websites. Shows email, phone, company data, and social profiles without leaving the browser.

Prospect Lists

Built-in database search with filters for job title, industry, company size, and location. Build targeted prospect lists and export directly to CRM or CSV.

CRM Integration

Native push to Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and Zoho from the Chrome extension. Enrich CRM records with Lusha data without manual copy-paste.

Bulk Enrichment

Upload a list of names and companies to enrich with direct dials and emails at scale. Useful for cleaning existing prospect lists or enriching event attendee data.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Best direct dial accuracy among consumer-grade contact tools
  • Chrome extension is the fastest way to get a phone number on LinkedIn
  • Simple, focused product that does what it promises without complexity
  • Affordable entry point for individual reps
  • Good EU data coverage compared to many US-centric competitors

What Could Be Better

  • Credit limits are strict — heavy prospectors burn through monthly allocation fast
  • Less database breadth than Apollo.io for overall contact volume
  • No built-in email sequencing — you'll need a separate sending tool
  • International coverage (APAC, LatAm) is weaker than North America and EU

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • SDRs who do heavy calling and need fast, accurate direct dial lookups
  • AEs who need to quickly find contact info for a specific person on LinkedIn
  • Teams that need good EU direct dial data without paying ZoomInfo pricing
  • Individuals needing affordable contact data without a full prospecting suite

Skip If You

  • Teams doing full prospecting workflows (Apollo.io is better value all-in-one)
  • Heavy email-focused prospecting without calling (Hunter.io is cheaper per email)
  • Organizations needing 1,000+ enrichments per month (Apollo or Clay at scale)
