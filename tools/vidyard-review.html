What Is Vidyard?

Vidyard is a video messaging platform built for sales that enables reps to record personalized video emails, screen shares, and demos to send as part of their outreach. Video emails increase reply rates significantly compared to plain text in the right contexts — particularly for warm prospects, complex product demos, and accounts where standing out matters. Vidyard tracks viewer engagement and notifies reps when prospects watch their videos.

Key Features

Video Email Recording

Record webcam, screen, or both from Chrome extension or desktop app. Create personalized video messages with a custom thumbnail that displays in the email body.

Viewer Analytics

Know exactly when a prospect watched your video, how much they watched, and whether they re-watched specific sections. Triggers follow-up at peak engagement moments.

Video CTA

Add calls-to-action directly inside the video player — meeting booking links, product URLs, or next steps appear when the video ends.

AI Script Generator

AI-generated video scripts based on prospect data and your value proposition. Reduces prep time before recording personalized messages.

Video Hub

Centralized library of shareable sales videos, product demos, and templates. Teams can share and reuse proven videos across the team.

CRM Integration

Native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Salesloft/Outreach. Video views auto-log to CRM records as engagement activity.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Free tier is actually useful with unlimited video recording
  • Video engagement notifications are genuinely valuable for follow-up timing
  • CTAs inside video players simplify the conversion path
  • AI script generator reduces recording preparation time

What Could Be Better

  • Video doesn't always render in every email client
  • Teams plan pricing jumps significantly from Pro
  • Video outreach works best for warm prospects — not all cold email contexts

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • SDRs doing outreach to warm prospects where personalized video adds value
  • AEs sending demo follow-ups and custom proposals via video
  • Teams targeting senior decision makers where standing out from email volume matters

Skip If You

  • Pure high-volume cold email operations where video adds friction
  • Teams whose prospects primarily use email clients that don't render video thumbnails

Limitations & Watch-Outs

No tool is perfect. Here are the downsides we found during testing.

  • Video selling requires team buy-in and behavior change
  • Free plan limitations push users toward paid tiers quickly
  • Analytics depth depends on viewer engagement with videos
  • Not all prospects prefer video messages over traditional email

When to Choose Vidyard: Use Cases

Vidyard works best in specific scenarios where its strengths align with your team's needs.

  • Sales reps using video to stand out in crowded inboxes
  • Teams wanting personalized video prospecting at scale
  • Enablement leaders creating training and coaching video libraries
  • Marketers measuring engagement with video content
