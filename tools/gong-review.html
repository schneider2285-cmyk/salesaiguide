What Is Gong?

Gong is a revenue intelligence platform that records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls using AI to surface deal intelligence, coaching insights, and revenue forecasting far deeper than CRM-based pipeline reports.

Note: Based on vendor docs and G2 reviews, not firsthand testing. See Gong on G2.

Key Features

AI Call Recording & Transcription

Records and transcribes Zoom, Teams, Meet calls with speaker ID.

Deal Intelligence & Risk Scoring

AI flags at-risk deals via engagement and sentiment tracking.

Sales Coaching with Scorecards

Call scorecards, snippet sharing, and team benchmarking.

Revenue Forecasting

AI forecasting from deal engagement, not CRM self-reports.

Market Intelligence

Competitor trends and objection patterns from call data.

CRM Integration (Auto-Logs Calls)

Bi-directional Salesforce/HubSpot sync with embedded insights.

Quick Verdict

✓ Best For

Sales organizations with 20+ reps that run a call-heavy sales motion and want AI-powered coaching, deal visibility, and forecast accuracy.

✗ Avoid If

Your team primarily sells via email, you have fewer than 10 reps, or you can't justify $200K+/year for conversation intelligence.

Bottom Line

Gong is the gold standard in conversation intelligence — nothing else comes close on AI depth and deal insights. The tradeoff is enterprise-level pricing with zero public transparency.

Pricing Breakdown

TL;DR: No public pricing. Expect $100-150/user/mo with annual contracts. Enterprise deals start $200K+.

⚠ Pricing Gotchas

  • No public pricing — must talk to sales for a quote
  • Typical contracts $100-150/user/mo with annual commitment
  • Minimum seat commitments are standard
  • Implementation fees ($10K-30K) often not disclosed upfront
  • 100 reps at $150/mo = $180K/year — scales linearly

What Gong Is Great At

  • Best-in-class call analysis: Unmatched conversation understanding and pattern recognition
  • Deal risk scoring: AI flags at-risk deals based on engagement, not rep sentiment
  • Coaching with snippets and scorecards: Share specific call moments and benchmark performance
  • Revenue forecasting: Predictions based on buyer engagement, not CRM self-reports
  • Market intelligence: Competitor trends and messaging effectiveness across all calls

Where Gong Breaks at Scale

  • Extremely expensive: Easily $200K+/year for mid-size teams
  • Opaque pricing: No way to plan costs without a sales call
  • Rep resistance to recording: Cultural adoption is a real challenge
  • Call-dependent: Email-first motions get zero value
  • RevOps configuration required: Real ROI needs custom deal board and coaching setup

Who Should NOT Buy Gong

  • Email-first sales teams: Zero value if reps aren't on calls
  • Small teams under 10 reps: Cost doesn't justify insights at this scale
  • No coaching culture: Without manager adoption, it's an expensive call recorder
  • Budget-conscious startups: Fireflies.ai or Avoma cover basics at 20% of cost
  • Teams needing only transcription: Fireflies.ai or Otter.ai are dramatically cheaper

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Gong cost for small teams?

No public pricing. Expect $100-150/user/month with annual contracts and seat minimums. A 10-person team pays $15K-25K/year. Implementation fees are additional.

How does Gong compare to Chorus?

Gong leads in AI depth, UX, and deal intelligence. Chorus offers tighter ZoomInfo integration. Gong has a higher G2 rating (4.8 vs 4.5) and broader adoption.

How do you justify the ROI on Gong?

ROI comes from win rate improvement (5-15%), faster rep ramp (30-50% reduction via call libraries), and better forecast accuracy. Teams with 20+ reps and coaching culture see strongest returns.
