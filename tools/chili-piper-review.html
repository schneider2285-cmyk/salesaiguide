What Is Chili Piper?

Chili Piper is a revenue acceleration platform that specializes in routing inbound leads to the right sales rep and booking meetings instantly from web forms. While Calendly focuses on outbound scheduling links, Chili Piper's primary use case is inbound qualification — when someone fills out a form on your website, Chili Piper qualifies them, routes to the right rep, and books the meeting in real time before the prospect leaves the page.

Key Features

Instant Booker (Form Concierge)

Qualifies web form submissions in real time, routes to the right rep, and presents a scheduling interface immediately after form completion. Dramatically reduces speed-to-lead.

Salesforce-Native Routing

Routes leads based on Salesforce territory assignments, ownership rules, and custom logic. The most sophisticated Salesforce routing in the category.

Round Robin & Routing Rules

Complex routing rules based on company size, geography, deal value, product interest, or any CRM data. Conditional routing for different qualification scenarios.

Distro (Lead Distribution)

Automatic lead distribution from Salesforce and other sources to the right rep based on ownership, territory, and custom logic — not just from web forms.

Handoff Scheduling

Enables SDRs to book meetings on behalf of AEs in real-time during calls or via email, with calendar availability shown and confirmed in seconds.

Analytics

Inbound speed-to-lead reporting, meeting booking rate analytics, and routing accuracy tracking for revenue operations teams.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Deep Salesforce-native integration beats Calendly significantly

What Could Be Better

  • Pricing adds up — full platform requires combining multiple products

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • Enterprise and mid-market teams with complex Salesforce routing needs

Skip If You
