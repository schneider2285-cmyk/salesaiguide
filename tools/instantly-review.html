Pricing Breakdown

TL;DR: $37/mo unlimited accounts. $97/mo for 25K leads + API. Enterprise custom.

⚠ Pricing Gotchas

  • Lead finder is a separate add-on, not included in sending plans
  • Growth plan's 1K active lead limit is restrictive beyond solo SDRs
  • Separate email domains/mailboxes ($2-5/mo each) on top of price
  • CRM beyond HubSpot/Salesforce/Pipedrive needs Zapier ($20+/mo)

What Instantly Is Great At

  • Unlimited sending accounts: Zero per-seat charges — #1 reason agencies choose Instantly
  • Deliverability management: Auto-warmup, sender rotation, and real-time dashboard
  • Price-to-value ratio: $37/mo with unlimited accounts — 40-60% cheaper than competitors
  • Unified inbox (Unibox): All replies from dozens of accounts in one view
  • Simple campaign builder: Easy multi-step sequences with A/B testing

Where Instantly Breaks at Scale

  • No built-in data: Must source leads externally, adding cost
  • Basic analytics: Limited campaign analytics and attribution
  • Limited CRM integrations: Only 3 native connectors; rest needs Zapier
  • Email-only channel: No LinkedIn or phone capability
  • DNS setup burden: SPF/DKIM/DMARC across many domains is tedious

Who Should NOT Buy Instantly

  • Low-volume senders: Under 100 emails/day makes this overkill
  • Enterprise security teams: No SSO, audit logs, or SOC 2
  • Multichannel sellers: Email only — no LinkedIn or phone
  • Teams wanting analytics depth: Reporting is basic vs. Salesloft or HubSpot

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Instantly.ai good for agencies?

Yes, Instantly is widely used by outreach agencies because of unlimited email accounts at no extra cost. You can manage multiple client campaigns from one dashboard and rotate sending across domains. The Hypergrowth plan ($97/mo) is designed for agencies running 25K+ active leads.

How does Instantly compare to Lemlist?

Instantly is cheaper ($37/mo vs $59/mo), allows unlimited email accounts (Lemlist charges per seat), and focuses on deliverability. Lemlist offers more personalization features like dynamic images and landing pages. Instantly is better for volume; Lemlist is better for creative campaigns.

Does Instantly.ai have a built-in contact database?

No. Instantly is a sending and deliverability platform. You need to bring your own lead lists from tools like Apollo, Clay, or ZoomInfo. Instantly added a lead finder in 2025, but it's a separate add-on, not included in base plans.
