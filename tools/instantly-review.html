What Is Instantly.ai?

Instantly.ai is a cold email platform built specifically for sending high-volume outreach without destroying your sender reputation. Unlike traditional ESP platforms, Instantly lets you connect unlimited email accounts and rotate sending across them to stay under spam radar.

After testing it for 6 months sending 2,000+ emails daily, I can confirm it actually delivers on this promise — which is rare in this category.

Pros & Cons (After Real Testing)

✓ What's Great

  • Actually works for deliverability: I've maintained 85%+ inbox rates sending 2K emails/day for 6 months straight
  • Unlimited accounts = unlimited scale: No per-seat pricing means you can add accounts as needed
  • Warmup is set-and-forget: Auto-warmup actually keeps accounts healthy without babysitting
  • Unibox saves hours: Managing replies across 10 accounts in one inbox is a game-changer
  • Price is unbeatable: Competitors charge 3-5x more for the same features
  • Fast support: Response times under 2 hours in my experience

✗ What Could Be Better

  • Learning curve on setup: Takes 2-3 hours to properly configure your first campaign
  • Limited native integrations: Zapier required for most CRMs besides the big 3
  • Analytics could be deeper: Basic reporting is fine but power users want more
  • No built-in prospecting: You need to bring your own list (use Clay or Apollo)
  • Template library is meh: Generic examples, you'll write your own anyway
