What Is Chorus?

Chorus is a conversation intelligence platform acquired by ZoomInfo in 2021. It records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls, providing deal intelligence and coaching tools similar to Gong but at typically lower price points. Its primary competitive positioning is as an integrated add-on for ZoomInfo customers rather than a standalone best-in-class alternative.

Key Features

Call Recording & Transcription

Automatic recording and AI transcription across Zoom, Teams, and phone systems. Speaker-labeled transcripts with keyword flagging and searchable library.

AI-Driven Insights

Conversation analytics surfacing key moments: competitor mentions, pricing discussions, objections, and next steps. Automated deal health scoring based on call patterns.

Coaching Tools

Rep performance analytics showing talk ratio, longest monologue, patience scores, and question frequency. Manager-facing coaching dashboards with custom scorecards.

CRM Integration

Deep Salesforce and HubSpot integration with activity auto-logging. ZoomInfo contact enrichment links conversation data to prospect profiles.

ZoomInfo Integration

Native data sharing with ZoomInfo — conversation insights overlay on ZoomInfo contact and company records for enriched buyer context.

Deal Intelligence

Deal velocity tracking, engagement scoring, and pipeline risk signals derived from conversation patterns and CRM activity data.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • Native ZoomInfo integration enriches conversation data with contact intelligence
  • Typically lower cost than Gong for similar conversation intelligence features
  • Easy bundling with ZoomInfo for unified vendor relationship
  • Solid coaching tools with rep performance scorecards
  • Strong Salesforce activity logging and deal sync

What Could Be Better

  • Less depth in deal intelligence than Gong's platform
  • Custom pricing requires sales engagement before evaluation
  • Best value only for existing ZoomInfo customers
  • Competitive disadvantage vs. Gong in standalone evaluation

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • Organizations already on ZoomInfo looking for bundled conversation intelligence
  • Teams that want Gong-like features at lower cost and are in ZoomInfo's ecosystem
  • Sales managers who need structured coaching scorecards for rep development

Skip If You

  • Teams not already on ZoomInfo — evaluate Gong or Fireflies instead
  • Enterprise orgs needing the deepest deal intelligence (Gong is better)
  • Small teams without budget for enterprise-tier pricing

Limitations & Watch-Outs

No tool is perfect. Here are the downsides we found during testing.

  • Now part of ZoomInfo — requires ZoomInfo subscription for full access
  • Less third-party integration support than standalone competitors
  • Interface can feel dated compared to newer conversation intelligence tools
  • Limited functionality outside of Zoom ecosystem

When to Choose Chorus: Use Cases

Chorus works best in specific scenarios where its strengths align with your team's needs.

  • ZoomInfo customers wanting integrated conversation intelligence
  • Teams already using Zoom for most sales meetings
  • Organizations needing basic call recording and analysis
  • Sales managers wanting quick meeting summaries and action items

Final Verdict

4.2 / 5

Chorus is a solid conversation intelligence platform that makes the most sense for organizations already in the ZoomInfo ecosystem.

As a bundled addition to an existing ZoomInfo relationship, the pricing and integration advantages make Chorus worth considering. As a standalone choice, most teams are better served by Gong for enterprise depth or Fireflies.ai for budget-friendly meeting intelligence.
