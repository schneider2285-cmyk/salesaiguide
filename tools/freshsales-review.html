What Is Freshsales?

Freshsales is a CRM from Freshworks that positions itself as an AI-powered alternative to HubSpot and Pipedrive for small to mid-size businesses. It includes a built-in phone, email tracking, contact scoring, and Freddy AI for deal insights — all at prices starting with a free tier that competes directly with HubSpot's free CRM.

Key Features

AI Lead Scoring (Freddy AI)

Built-in AI scores leads and contacts based on engagement, firmographics, and behavior patterns. Prioritizes rep attention on the highest-converting prospects automatically.

Built-in Phone

Integrated VoIP phone with click-to-call, call recording, and activity logging directly from the CRM without a separate dialer.

Visual Pipeline

Drag-and-drop deal pipeline with customizable stages, deal values, and activity reminders. Multiple pipeline views for different products or sales motions.

Email Tracking & Sequences

Email open/click tracking, templates, and automated multi-step email sequences for prospect follow-up.

AI Deal Insights

Freddy AI surfaces deal risk signals, next best actions, and engagement recommendations based on deal activity patterns.

Workflow Automation

No-code automation builder for routing leads, updating deal stages, sending alerts, and triggering follow-up tasks based on CRM events.

Pros & Cons

What's Great

  • AI lead scoring included on $15/seat plan — best value in category
  • Built-in phone means no separate dialer purchase
  • Free tier with unlimited users is genuinely competitive with HubSpot
  • Easy setup and intuitive interface for small teams

What Could Be Better

  • Less ecosystem breadth than HubSpot
  • Reporting less sophisticated for complex analytics needs
  • Support quality inconsistent at lower plan levels
  • Less widely adopted — fewer integration partners than HubSpot/Salesforce

Who It's For

Perfect For

  • Small businesses (1-25 reps) wanting AI-powered CRM at affordable pricing
  • Teams that want built-in phone without paying for a separate dialer
  • Cost-conscious teams evaluating HubSpot vs. a more affordable alternative

Skip If You

  • Growing teams that need HubSpot's marketing automation ecosystem
  • Enterprise teams needing advanced Salesforce-level customization
