Our Testing Process

Here's how every tool gets reviewed:

  1. Real-world deployment — Set up in actual sales workflow, not a test environment
  2. 3–6 month minimum testing — Long enough to see if it actually works over time
  3. Metrics tracking — Measure reply rates, conversion rates, time saved, ROI
  4. Team feedback — Test with SDRs, AEs, and managers to get different perspectives
  5. Comparison testing — Direct head-to-head vs competitors in same category
  6. Honest write-up — Document what works, what doesn't, and who it's actually for
