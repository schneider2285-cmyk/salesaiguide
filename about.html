About Sales AI Guide

What this site is, how it works, and why it exists.

What This Site Is

Sales AI Guide is a decision engine for AI-powered sales tools. We synthesize evidence from 8 categories of public sources — review marketplaces, practitioner communities, video walkthroughs, vendor documentation, and more — into structured Decision Snapshots that answer the questions operators actually ask before buying.

We are not a review site. We don't claim to have personally tested every tool for months. We're a decision engine that saves you time by synthesizing scattered evidence into structured, comparable guidance instead of making you visit 10 different sites.

Where Our Data Comes From

  • Review marketplaces — G2, TrustRadius, Capterra, GetApp, Software Advice for sentiment patterns
  • Practitioner communities — Reddit, LinkedIn, RevGenius, Pavilion for real-world workflows and candid opinions
  • Video walkthroughs — YouTube practitioner demos and vendor walkthroughs for UX verification
  • Podcasts & interviews — Revenue and sales podcasts for implementation reality and decision criteria
  • Social signal — LinkedIn posts/comments and X/Twitter threads for emerging sentiment
  • Vendor documentation — Official docs, pricing pages, security/trust pages, integration directories, changelogs
  • Ecosystem marketplaces — Salesforce AppExchange, HubSpot Marketplace, Zapier, Chrome Web Store
  • Job posting signals — Stack co-occurrence patterns in sales job listings

We check this data regularly but can't guarantee it's always current. Pricing changes, features get added, ratings shift. When in doubt, check the vendor's site directly.

AI-Assisted Content

Let's be upfront: the descriptions, comparisons, and summaries on this site are AI-assisted. We use AI tools to help write and structure content based on vendor documentation and public data.

We think this is fine as long as we're honest about it (which we are, right here). The value we provide isn't in original prose—it's in organizing scattered information into a useful, comparable format.

How We Earn Money

This site earns revenue through affiliate links. When you click a link to a tool and sign up, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate links are formatted as: /go/[toolname]

We try to include affiliate links for every tool we list, not just the ones that pay the most. Our goal is to be a useful decision engine first. If the decision engine is useful, the affiliate revenue follows.

Who's Behind This

Sales AI Guide was built out of frustration with how hard it is to compare sales AI tools. Most "review" sites are just SEO plays with recycled vendor copy. We wanted something more straightforward.

This site is intentionally simple. No gated content, no mandatory email signups, no pop-ups. Just a decision engine you can actually use.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't accept payment for rankings or placement
  • We don't claim to have personally tested every tool
  • We don't fabricate subscriber counts or social proof
  • We don't write fake "I tested this for 6 months" narratives
