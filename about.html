Our Review Process

Every review combines direct operational knowledge with structured research:

  1. Hands-on experience — Core tools in my daily workflow are deployed in real sales environments and used over months
  2. Structured research — Official vendor documentation, changelog analysis, public feature comparisons, and verified pricing data
  3. User evidence — Aggregated feedback from G2, Capterra, and verified practitioner communities
  4. Practitioner perspective — Every evaluation is filtered through the lens of someone who actually sells for a living
  5. Comparison testing — Direct head-to-head analysis using consistent scoring criteria across categories
  6. Honest write-up — Document what works, what doesn't, and who it's actually for
