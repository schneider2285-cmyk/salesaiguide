Which Instantly Plan Should You Pick?

I'll keep this simple. Your plan choice comes down to how many leads you're working at any given time.

Solo founder or freelancer

Go with Growth ($37/month). You get unlimited email accounts and warmup, which is the whole point of Instantly. At 1,000 active leads, you can run meaningful campaigns without hitting the ceiling. Most solopreneurs won't need more than this for the first 6-12 months.

Small team (2-5 reps)

Growth ($37/month) still works. Since Instantly charges per workspace instead of per seat, a small team can share one Growth plan without paying per user. That's a massive cost advantage over tools like Lemlist or Outreach that bill per seat.

Growth team (5-15 reps)

Hypergrowth ($97/month). Once you're managing more than 1,000 active leads across multiple campaigns, the Hypergrowth plan is the obvious upgrade. The API access is particularly useful at this stage for piping data into your CRM or analytics stack. Advanced analytics help you figure out which sequences actually convert.

Large or enterprise team

Talk to their sales team. If you're running millions of emails per month across dozens of domains, the Enterprise plan gets you unlimited leads, dedicated support, and custom integrations. Pricing is negotiable.

Hidden Costs & Watch-Outs

Instantly's pricing is straightforward, but there are real costs beyond the sticker price that catch people off guard.

No built-in prospecting data

Instantly is a sending tool, not a data tool. You'll need a separate product for finding leads and getting verified emails. Most Instantly users pair it with Apollo (free-$49/month) or Clay ($149-800/month) for lead sourcing. Factor that into your total cost.

CRM integrations require Zapier

Instantly doesn't have native CRM integrations on the Growth plan. If you want to push data to HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive, you'll need Zapier or Make, which adds $20-50/month to your stack. The Hypergrowth plan's API access helps, but you still need someone technical to set it up.

Basic reporting on Growth

The Growth plan's analytics show opens and replies, but nothing deeper. If you need to know which sequences drive pipeline or revenue, you'll either need Hypergrowth or an external analytics tool.

Domain and mailbox costs

Unlimited email accounts sounds great, but you still need to buy the domains and Google Workspace/Outlook mailboxes. Budget roughly $12/year per domain and $6-7/month per mailbox. Running 10 accounts means an extra $60-70/month in infrastructure.

How Instantly Compares on Price

The cold outreach tool market has a wide price range. Here's how Instantly stacks up against the closest competitors.

Instantly vs Lemlist

Lemlist starts at $39/month per seat and goes up to $99/seat for their top tier. For a single user, the price difference is negligible. For a team of five, Instantly at $37-97/month total is dramatically cheaper than Lemlist at $195-495/month. Lemlist does include more multichannel features (LinkedIn steps, landing pages) that Instantly lacks. See our full Instantly vs Lemlist comparison.

Instantly vs Smartlead

Smartlead's pricing ($39-94/month) is nearly identical to Instantly's. The real difference is in the product: Smartlead's interface is rougher but offers slightly more customization on warmup settings. If you're choosing purely on price, it's a wash. The decision should come down to UX preference and feature specifics. Read our Instantly vs Smartlead breakdown.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Instantly cost per month?

Instantly's Growth plan costs $37/month and includes unlimited email accounts, unlimited warmup, and 1,000 active leads. The Hypergrowth plan is $97/month with 25,000 active leads, advanced analytics, and API access. Enterprise pricing is custom.

Does Instantly have a free plan?

No, Instantly doesn't offer a permanent free plan. They do offer a free trial that lets you test the platform before picking a paid plan. You'll need a credit card to start the trial.

Is Instantly worth the price?

If cold email is your primary outreach channel, yes. At $37/month for unlimited sending accounts, it's one of the best deals in the cold outreach space. The main gap is prospecting data—you'll need a separate tool for finding leads. Read our full Instantly review for a detailed breakdown.

How does Instantly pricing compare to Lemlist?

Instantly charges per workspace ($37-97/month) while Lemlist charges per seat ($39-99/month per user). For solo users, the cost is similar. For teams, Instantly is significantly cheaper because you're not paying per rep. See our full comparison.

What's included in the Instantly Growth plan?

The Growth plan ($37/month) includes unlimited email accounts, unlimited email warmup, 1,000 active leads, email sequence automation, and basic analytics. It does not include API access, advanced analytics, or team collaboration features—those require the Hypergrowth plan.
