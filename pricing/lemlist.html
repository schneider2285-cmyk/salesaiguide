What Does Lemlist Actually Cost?

Lemlist runs $39 to $99 per seat per month depending on features. That "per seat" part matters a lot — unlike Instantly or Smartlead, which charge flat monthly rates, Lemlist bills for every user on your account. A 10-person team on Email Pro is paying $690/month before you factor in anything else.

Email Pro at $69/seat is where most teams should start. Personalized images and AI-generated variables are what make Lemlist worth using over cheaper alternatives. If you only need basic email sequences without personalization, you're overpaying here — Instantly does that for $37/month flat.

All plans include Lemwarm (their email warming tool), which is a genuine perk. Most competitors charge extra for warmup or make you buy a separate tool. Lemlist bakes it in at every tier.

Lemlist Plans & Pricing Breakdown

Email Starter — $39/seat/month

The entry point. You get email sequences, Lemwarm for deliverability, and basic personalization with custom variables. Each seat comes with 1 sending email address.

This plan works if you're a solo rep testing cold outreach for the first time. But honestly, at $39/seat with only basic personalization, you're paying a premium for the Lemlist brand. Instantly's Growth plan at $37/month gives you unlimited email accounts and more sending volume.

  • Email sequences with basic personalization
  • Lemwarm email warming included
  • 1 sending email per seat
  • Basic reporting and analytics
  • Standard support

Email Pro — $69/seat/month (Most Popular)

This is the plan that justifies choosing Lemlist over the competition. You get everything in Starter plus personalized images (your prospect's logo or headshot embedded in emails), AI-powered personalization variables, and advanced analytics.

I recommend Email Pro for agencies and reps who are doing targeted, lower-volume outreach where each email needs to feel handcrafted. The image personalization genuinely increases reply rates — I've seen 15-20% lifts in A/B tests when using personalized images vs. plain text.

  • Everything in Email Starter
  • Personalized images and dynamic landing pages
  • AI-generated personalization variables
  • Advanced analytics and A/B testing
  • CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive)
  • Priority support

Multichannel — $99/seat/month

Everything in Email Pro plus LinkedIn automation, phone task sequences, and conditional branching in your workflows. This is Lemlist's pitch for teams that want email + LinkedIn + calling in one platform.

The LinkedIn automation works, but I have reservations. Lemlist uses browser-based automation that LinkedIn actively detects. I've seen accounts get restricted after 2-3 weeks of aggressive LinkedIn sequences. If LinkedIn outreach is critical to your workflow, budget for the risk of temporary account restrictions.

  • Everything in Email Pro
  • LinkedIn profile visits, connection requests, and messages
  • Phone call task sequences
  • Conditional branching (if/then workflow logic)
  • API access
  • Advanced team management

Which Lemlist Plan Should You Pick?

Solo rep testing cold email

Email Starter ($39/seat/month). Use the 14-day free trial first. If you're just sending basic email sequences without image personalization, honestly consider Instantly instead — it's cheaper for pure volume plays.

Small team (2-5 reps) focused on quality outreach

Email Pro ($69/seat/month). This is the sweet spot. At $138-345/month for 2-5 seats, you're getting the personalization features that actually differentiate Lemlist. The image personalization alone can justify the premium over cheaper tools if your outreach is targeted enough.

Growth team needing multichannel

Multichannel ($99/seat/month). Only if you genuinely need LinkedIn automation and phone sequences in the same platform. At $495-990/month for 5-10 seats, make sure you're actually using all three channels. Most teams I talk to buy Multichannel and end up only using email anyway.

Enterprise or large team (15+ reps)

Contact Lemlist sales. At 15+ seats, you're looking at $1,035-1,485/month minimum. At that spend level, negotiate. Lemlist offers volume discounts on annual contracts that aren't advertised on their pricing page. Push for 20-30% off list price.

Hidden Costs & What Lemlist Doesn't Tell You

Per-seat pricing scales fast

This is the biggest gotcha. A 10-rep sales team on Email Pro pays $690/month. On Multichannel, that's $990/month. Meanwhile, Instantly charges $37-97/month total regardless of team size. If you're scaling a team, do the math on per-seat vs. flat-rate pricing before committing.

Limited sending emails per seat

Email Starter gives you 1 sending email per seat. Email Pro bumps that up, but you're still limited compared to tools like Instantly that offer unlimited email accounts. If you need high sending volume across multiple domains, you'll feel this constraint.

LinkedIn automation carries real risk

The Multichannel plan's LinkedIn features use browser automation that LinkedIn actively monitors. I've personally seen accounts get temporarily restricted, and I've heard from users who lost LinkedIn accounts entirely. Budget for this risk — it's not a dealbreaker, but don't run aggressive LinkedIn sequences and expect zero consequences.

Image personalization can trigger spam filters

Personalized images are Lemlist's signature feature, but embedding images in cold emails can hurt deliverability. Some email providers flag image-heavy cold emails. Test carefully with small batches before rolling out image personalization across your entire campaign list.

Smaller contact database

Unlike Apollo or ZoomInfo, Lemlist doesn't have a massive built-in contact database. You'll need to bring your own leads or pair Lemlist with a separate prospecting tool. That's an additional cost most people don't account for upfront.

Lemlist Pricing vs. Competitors

Lemlist vs. Instantly

Instantly charges $37/month (Growth) to $97/month (Hypergrowth) as flat fees — no per-seat pricing. For a solo user, Lemlist Email Starter ($39) and Instantly Growth ($37) are nearly identical in cost. But for a team of 5 on Email Pro, you're paying $345/month for Lemlist vs. $37-97 for Instantly. Instantly wins on pure volume and cost. Lemlist wins on personalization quality.

Lemlist vs. Smartlead

Smartlead runs $39/month (Basic) to $94/month (Pro) with unlimited email accounts and no per-seat fees. Like Instantly, it's built for volume. Smartlead lacks Lemlist's image personalization and multichannel features, but if your strategy is high-volume email outreach, Smartlead gives you more sending capacity for less money.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Lemlist cost?

Lemlist costs $39/seat/month for Email Starter, $69/seat/month for Email Pro, and $99/seat/month for Multichannel. All plans are billed per seat, so a team of 5 on Email Pro runs $345/month. Annual billing saves roughly 20% compared to monthly.

Does Lemlist have a free trial?

Yes, Lemlist offers a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get access to most features during the trial, including personalized images and sequence builders. I recommend setting up at least one full campaign during the trial to properly evaluate deliverability.

Is Lemlist worth $69/month?

Email Pro at $69/seat is worth it if you're doing targeted, personalized outreach where image personalization and AI variables improve your reply rates. If you're running high-volume campaigns with basic personalization, you'll get more value from Instantly or Smartlead at lower cost. The value equation depends entirely on your outreach style.

How does Lemlist pricing compare to Instantly?

Instantly charges flat monthly rates ($37-97/mo) while Lemlist charges per seat ($39-99/seat/mo). For solo users the cost is similar. For teams, the gap widens fast. A 10-person team on Lemlist Email Pro pays $690/month; the same team on Instantly pays $37-97/month. See our full Instantly vs. Lemlist comparison.

Does Lemlist include LinkedIn automation?

Only on the Multichannel plan at $99/seat/month. Email Starter and Email Pro are email-only. The LinkedIn automation includes profile visits, connection requests, and messages, but be aware that LinkedIn actively detects and restricts accounts using browser-based automation tools.
