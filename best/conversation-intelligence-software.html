What to Look For in Conversation Intelligence Software

The first thing I evaluate is transcription accuracy and speed. If the transcript is wrong, every downstream insight is wrong. The best tools deliver speaker-labeled transcripts within minutes of a call ending, with 90%+ accuracy across standard English dialects. Accuracy drops noticeably with heavy accents, background noise, or multi-speaker crosstalk—so test with your actual call recordings before committing.

Second is depth of analytics. Basic tools give you talk ratios and keyword mentions. Enterprise platforms like Gong go further with deal health scoring, pipeline risk alerts, competitive intelligence dashboards, and AI-driven forecasting. The question is whether your team needs analytics that inform board-level decisions or just needs searchable meeting notes so reps stop losing track of what prospects said.

Third is CRM integration quality. Every tool on this list syncs with Salesforce and HubSpot, but the depth varies. Auto-logging calls and meeting notes saves reps 30+ minutes per day of manual CRM entry. The best platforms match recordings to the correct contact and deal records without manual tagging, which eliminates the "it is in Gong but not in Salesforce" problem that plagues teams with poor adoption.

Finally, coaching infrastructure matters for managers. Scorecards, talk pattern analysis, objection handling metrics, and curated call playlists for onboarding are what separate a recording tool from a coaching platform. If you manage more than five reps, you want structured coaching data—not just a library of recordings you will never have time to review. Our Top Picks

Gong — Best Overall for Enterprise

Gong is the category leader because it treats every customer interaction—calls, emails, meetings—as a data point in a larger revenue picture. The deal intelligence feature aggregates signals from every conversation into unified deal timelines with engagement scoring, and risk alerts flag deals where buyer engagement is declining or key stakeholders have gone silent. For organizations running complex, multi-stakeholder sales with 50+ reps, this level of pipeline visibility is genuinely transformative.

The coaching tools are where Gong pulls ahead of every competitor I tested. Talk ratio analytics, question pattern analysis, and objection handling scoring give managers concrete metrics to anchor coaching conversations instead of relying on anecdotal call shadowing. The competitive intelligence module tracks competitor mentions across every recorded conversation and aggregates trend dashboards showing which competitors appear, in which deal stages, and how those mentions correlate with win/loss outcomes. Auto-CRM logging eliminates manual activity entry for reps, with deep Salesforce and HubSpot bidirectional sync that keeps pipeline data consistent.

Pricing is not public. All plans require custom quotes with annual commitments. Estimated pricing is $100-150/user/month for mid-market teams. Enterprise plans with advanced competitive intelligence and dedicated CSM are priced individually. Revenue intelligence features like forecasting may be add-on modules with separate pricing.

Limitations: Gong pricing excludes teams under 30-50 reps—the investment does not pencil out at small scale. Minimum seat requirements typically start at 15+. Implementation requires significant organizational effort and change management, with 4-6 weeks of recorded calls needed before AI insights become statistically meaningful. Annual contracts lock you in before proving value in your specific environment. Two-party consent states require explicit recording disclosure, so your legal team needs to approve call flows before deployment.

Fireflies.ai — Best Budget Option

Fireflies.ai makes conversation intelligence accessible to teams that cannot justify Gong's enterprise pricing. Setup takes about five minutes: connect your Google or Outlook calendar, and the Fireflies bot automatically joins scheduled meetings on Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, or Webex. After each call, you get a full speaker-labeled transcript with 90%+ accuracy, an AI-generated summary highlighting key discussion points and action items, and everything is searchable across your entire meeting library.

The free tier is genuinely useful—800 minutes of transcription per month covers roughly 25-30 meetings at average call lengths, which handles most individual rep workloads without paying anything. No credit card required. The Pro plan at $10/month/seat removes the minute cap and adds video recording and unlimited transcription. The Business plan at $19/month/seat adds team analytics and API access. For context, that is 10-15x cheaper per seat than Gong for the core meeting intelligence use case.

Smart search across your entire meeting library is particularly valuable for account executives who need to reference details from earlier conversations before follow-up calls. You can search across months of recorded calls by keyword, topic, or speaker and find any moment in seconds. Summary templates can be customized per meeting type, so discovery calls and demos produce different output formats automatically. Meeting playlists let managers curate collections of call segments for onboarding training and objection handling examples.

Limitations: Fireflies' analytics are much less sophisticated than Gong for deal intelligence. There is no native deal health scoring, pipeline risk alerts, or revenue forecasting. Coaching tools are basic compared to enterprise platforms—you get talk time, question rates, and filler word frequency, but not the structured scorecard infrastructure managers need at scale. Transcription accuracy drops with heavy accents or background noise, and the meeting bot notification can feel intrusive to some prospects.

Chorus — Best for ZoomInfo Customers

Chorus is a conversation intelligence platform acquired by ZoomInfo in 2021, and that acquisition defines its best use case. If your organization already runs on ZoomInfo for prospecting data, Chorus gives you conversation intelligence at a lower cost than Gong with native data sharing—conversation insights overlay directly on ZoomInfo contact and company records for enriched buyer context that no standalone tool can match.

The feature set covers core conversation intelligence needs: automatic recording and AI transcription across Zoom, Teams, and phone systems with speaker-labeled transcripts and keyword flagging. Coaching tools include rep performance analytics showing talk ratio, longest monologue, patience scores, and question frequency, with manager-facing dashboards and custom scorecards. Deal intelligence tracks velocity, engagement scoring, and pipeline risk signals derived from conversation patterns and CRM activity data. Strong Salesforce activity logging and deal sync keeps your CRM current without manual rep effort.

Pricing is custom with no public numbers. Generally priced competitively below Gong for organizations in the ZoomInfo ecosystem. Contracts are typically annual with volume discounts for larger teams. The best pricing is usually achieved by bundling Chorus with an existing ZoomInfo subscription. Check the official pricing page for the latest details.

Limitations: Chorus has less depth in deal intelligence than Gong, and its interface can feel dated compared to newer tools. The product roadmap is tied to ZoomInfo's priorities, so feature requests get filtered through a larger platform strategy you cannot influence. Third-party integration support is more limited than standalone competitors—connecting to non-ZoomInfo tools often requires middleware. Call data export options are restricted, requiring API work for external analysis or compliance audits. Teams not already on ZoomInfo lose the primary integration advantage and should evaluate Gong or Fireflies instead.

Clari — Best for Revenue Operations

Clari approaches the conversation intelligence problem from a different angle than the other three tools on this list. Where Gong, Fireflies, and Chorus focus on what happens during calls, Clari focuses on what happens to your pipeline after calls—using AI to analyze CRM data, rep activity, and historical patterns to predict which deals will close and when. For revenue operations teams and CROs who need board-level forecast accuracy, Clari is the strongest option in this category.

The AI pipeline forecasting replaces manual rollup processes with data-driven predictions based on observable behaviors. Deal inspection surfaces at-risk opportunities before reps flag them in forecast calls by analyzing engagement scores, activity history, and risk factors. Multi-level forecasting supports complex organizational hierarchies—rep, team, region, segment, and company-wide rollups in a single platform. CRM hygiene automation identifies stale or incomplete records and prompts reps to update critical fields, which is valuable because forecast accuracy is only as good as your underlying data quality.

Pricing is custom with no public numbers. Typically $50-100/seat/month based on team size and features selected. Expect a multi-week evaluation process including a proof-of-concept with your actual CRM data before receiving final pricing. The Revenue + Groove plan bundles sales engagement features including email sequences and activity capture for teams that want both forecasting and outreach in one vendor.

Limitations: Forecast accuracy depends entirely on CRM data quality—garbage in, garbage out. Implementation requires 6-8 weeks with a dedicated Salesforce admin and executive sponsor. ROI is hardest to prove for teams under 20 reps because the AI needs sufficient deal volume to identify meaningful patterns. Clari offers less conversation coaching capability than Gong, so teams that primarily need call recording and rep development should look at Gong or even Fireflies instead. No self-serve evaluation—you must commit to a sales process before seeing the product with your data.

The Bottom Line

For enterprise sales organizations with 50+ reps who need the full picture—deal intelligence, forecasting, coaching infrastructure, and competitive intelligence—Gong is the clear choice at $100-150/user/month. The investment is significant, but the pipeline visibility is unmatched. For individuals and small teams who want meeting transcription and AI summaries without the enterprise price tag, Fireflies.ai offers the best value in the category—the free tier alone covers most solo rep workflows, and $10/month/seat gets you unlimited transcription.

If your organization already runs on ZoomInfo, Chorus gives you conversation intelligence at a lower cost than Gong with native data enrichment that no standalone tool can replicate. And for revenue operations leaders where forecast accuracy is a board-level concern, Clari delivers AI-driven pipeline predictions that replace manual rollup processes at $50-100/seat/month. Frequently Asked Questions

What is conversation intelligence software?

Conversation intelligence software records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls and meetings using AI. It surfaces insights like talk ratios, competitor mentions, objection patterns, and deal health signals. Sales teams use it for coaching, pipeline visibility, and forecasting accuracy. Tools range from budget meeting transcription like Fireflies.ai at $10/month to enterprise revenue intelligence platforms like Gong at $100-150/user/month.

How much does conversation intelligence software cost?

Pricing varies widely. Fireflies.ai offers a free tier with 800 minutes/month and paid plans from $10/month/seat. Enterprise platforms like Gong run $100-150/user/month with custom quotes and annual contracts. Chorus offers custom pricing typically below Gong when bundled with ZoomInfo. Clari runs approximately $50-100/seat/month for its revenue operations platform.

What is the best free conversation intelligence tool?

Fireflies.ai offers the best free tier in conversation intelligence. The free plan includes 800 minutes of transcription per month, AI-generated meeting summaries, smart search across your meeting library, and three CRM integrations. No credit card required. The 800 minutes cover roughly 25-30 meetings per month, which is enough for most individual sales reps.

Do I need Gong or is a cheaper tool sufficient?

It depends on team size and what you need. If you have 50+ reps and need deal intelligence, pipeline forecasting, competitive intelligence, and structured coaching analytics, Gong justifies the enterprise investment. If you need meeting transcription, searchable call notes, and basic coaching for a small team, Fireflies.ai at $10/month/seat handles the core use case at a fraction of the cost. Teams under 30 reps rarely get enough ROI from Gong to justify the pricing.

Can conversation intelligence software integrate with my CRM?

Yes. All four tools I tested integrate with Salesforce and HubSpot. Gong offers deep bidirectional CRM sync with auto-logged calls, emails, and meetings. Fireflies.ai syncs meeting notes and transcripts automatically to HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive. Chorus adds native ZoomInfo contact enrichment on top of CRM integration. Clari focuses on pulling CRM pipeline data for AI-driven forecasting and deal inspection.
