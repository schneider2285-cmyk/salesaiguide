Editorial Policy

How we choose tools, how we make money, and what we stand behind.

What SalesAI Guide Is

SalesAI Guide is an independent research site focused on helping sales teams and individual sales professionals choose the right AI tools.

We are not a review warehouse. We are a decision engine.

Our goal is simple: make it easier to decide what to use and what to avoid.

How We Make Money

Some links on this site are affiliate links.

If you click a link and purchase a product, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate relationships do not influence our scores or rankings.

How We Choose and Evaluate Tools

We evaluate tools using a structured scoring model and decision framework.

We base our evaluations on:

  • Public user review sentiment (patterns and themes)
  • Practitioner discussions in sales and RevOps communities
  • Public demo videos and walkthroughs
  • Vendor documentation (features, security, integrations, pricing)
  • Public ecosystem signals (marketplaces and integrations)
  • Public job-posting patterns that show real-world usage

We do not copy reviews or fabricate experiences.

We synthesize public signals into structured guidance.

How We Score Tools

Each tool is evaluated across six core dimensions:

  • Enterprise Fit
  • Ease of Adoption
  • AI Depth
  • Integration Depth
  • Pricing Transparency
  • Scalability

We also calculate two overall scores:

  • Org Fit Score (for team-level buyers)
  • Individual Rep Value Score (for personal tool buyers)

You can read more about our scoring approach on the How We Score page.

Freshness and Verification

Each tool page includes a "Last verified" date.

We periodically review pricing, features, and positioning — especially on high-traffic pages.

If something changes, we update it.

Corrections

If you believe something is inaccurate, please contact us.

We review correction requests seriously and update factual errors when needed.

No Pay-to-Rank

We do not accept payment to improve rankings or scores.

If a page includes sponsored placement or partnership disclosure, it will be clearly labeled.

Our Approach

We aim to be:

Clear. Structured. Honest.

We highlight strengths — and we call out limitations.

If a tool breaks at scale or isn't right for certain teams, we say so.

Our focus is decision clarity, not hype.
