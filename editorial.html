Editorial Guidelines

How we select tools, aggregate data, and maintain accuracy.

How Tools Are Selected

We include AI-powered tools that serve B2B sales teams across the pipeline — from prospecting to closing. A tool is considered for inclusion if it meets these criteria:

  • Active product: The tool is currently available and maintained (not sunset or in beta)
  • B2B sales focus: It serves sales teams specifically, not general marketing or CRM-only
  • Public review data: It has ratings on at least one major review platform (G2, Capterra, or TrustRadius)
  • AI component: It uses artificial intelligence or machine learning as a core feature, not just basic automation

We do not accept payment for inclusion. All tools listed meet the criteria above regardless of whether they have an affiliate program.

Data Sources

We aggregate data from multiple public sources to build a more complete picture:

  • G2: Star ratings, review counts, category rankings, and reviewer sentiment themes
  • Capterra: Ratings, review volumes, and feature comparisons
  • TrustRadius: trScore ratings and detailed reviewer feedback patterns
  • Product Hunt: Launch data, upvotes, and community reception for newer tools
  • Vendor websites: Pricing pages, feature lists, documentation, and integration directories

When we display a single rating (e.g., "4.8/5"), it is the G2 rating unless otherwise noted. We chose G2 as the primary source because it has the largest volume of verified B2B software reviews.

Verification and Update Cadence

We verify tool data on a monthly cycle:

  • Pricing: Checked monthly against vendor pricing pages
  • Ratings: Updated monthly from G2 and cross-referenced with Capterra/TrustRadius
  • Features: Reviewed when vendors announce major updates or version changes
  • Availability: Tools that are sunset, acquired, or significantly changed are updated or removed

Each tool listing displays a "Verified" date showing when the data was last confirmed. Despite our efforts, pricing and features can change without notice. We always recommend verifying on the vendor's website before making a purchase decision.

"Best For" Designations

We assign "Best for X" badges to tools based on defensible criteria:

  • Feature differentiation: The tool has a clear, documented advantage in that area (e.g., "Best for Volume" = unlimited email accounts)
  • Review platform positioning: G2 category rankings and reviewer sentiment support the designation
  • Pricing alignment: Budget designations reflect actual pricing (e.g., "Best Budget" = lowest entry price in category)

These are editorial designations, not paid placements. Tools cannot pay for a "Best for" badge. If you believe a designation is inaccurate, contact us and we'll review it.

AI-Assisted Content

We use AI tools to assist with writing tool descriptions, comparisons, and guide content. Specifically:

  • Tool descriptions are drafted using AI based on vendor documentation and public review data
  • Comparison content synthesizes information from multiple review platforms
  • All AI-generated content is reviewed for accuracy against source data

We don't hide this. The value of Sales AI Guide is in curation and organization — pulling scattered data into one structured, comparable format. The AI assists with writing; the data comes from real review platforms and vendor sites.

Affiliate Relationships

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you sign up for a tool through one of these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Important points:

  • Affiliate relationships do not influence ratings, rankings, or "Best for" designations
  • We list tools with and without affiliate programs using the same criteria
  • Affiliate links are marked with disclosure text and use the /go/ URL format

For complete details, see our affiliate disclosure page.

Report an Error

Found incorrect pricing, an outdated rating, or a factual error? We want to fix it. Email hello@salesaiguide.com with the page URL and what needs correcting. We typically update within 48 hours.
