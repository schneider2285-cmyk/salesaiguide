Affiliate Disclosure

How this site earns money and what you should know before clicking any link.

The Short Version

Sales AI Guide earns money through affiliate links. When you click a link on this site and sign up for a tool, the vendor may pay us a referral commission. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the vendor's website.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links?

Any link formatted as salesaiguide.com/go/[toolname] is an affiliate link. These redirect to the vendor's website with our tracking code attached.

We have affiliate relationships with most tools listed on this site, including:

  • Instantly.ai
  • Clay
  • Apollo.io
  • Gong
  • Lavender
  • HubSpot
  • Outreach
  • Salesloft
  • And others listed in the directory

Not every tool listed has an affiliate program. Tools without affiliate links are still included because the directory is more useful when it's comprehensive.

Where Our Data Comes From

This is important context for evaluating anything on this site. Our evaluations synthesize evidence from multiple public source types:

  • Review platform ratings & themes — Synthesized from G2, Capterra, TrustRadius, and Trustpilot. We surface recurring patterns across review sets, not individual opinions.
  • Community signals — Sourced from Reddit threads, LinkedIn discussions, and practitioner communities.
  • Video walkthroughs — Sourced from YouTube demos and recorded product walkthroughs.
  • Pricing — Pulled from vendor pricing pages. We check these periodically but prices change. Always verify on the vendor's site before purchasing.
  • Feature lists & security posture — Sourced from vendor documentation, marketing pages, and security/trust pages.
  • Scores, descriptions & comparisons — AI-assisted synthesis of the above sources. Not firsthand product reviews.

What This Site Is (and Is Not)

Sales AI Guide is an affiliate-supported decision engine that synthesizes public evidence from multiple source types into structured tool evaluations. To be completely clear about what that means:

  • This is not a review site based on hands-on testing of every tool
  • This is not an independent research publication
  • Our scores and evaluations reflect synthesized evidence from public sources — not firsthand product testing
  • Descriptions are AI-assisted summaries of public evidence, disclosed openly

Our value is in synthesizing scattered public evidence — from review platforms, practitioner communities, video demos, and vendor documentation — into structured, comparable decision guidance. For primary source research, we recommend visiting G2, Capterra, or requesting demos directly from vendors.

Does Affiliate Revenue Affect What We List?

No. We list tools based on category relevance and G2 rating, not commission rates. Tools without affiliate programs are included alongside tools that pay us.

We do not:

  • Accept payment to rank tools higher
  • Remove tools because they don't have affiliate programs
  • Write more favorably about tools that pay higher commissions

FTC Compliance

This disclosure complies with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). Material connections between Sales AI Guide and the companies whose products are listed are disclosed here and throughout the site.
